# Resource Well

## Resource Well  
  
[ ](/wiki/File:Resource_Well.png "Resource Well.png")

### Unlocked by

[Tier 8](/wiki/Tier_8 "Tier 8") \- Advanced Aluminum Production

**Resource Wells** are specific locations in the world from which raw [fluid](/wiki/Fluid "Fluid") resources can be extracted: [](/wiki/Nitrogen_Gas "Nitrogen Gas") [Nitrogen Gas](/wiki/Nitrogen_Gas "Nitrogen Gas"), [](/wiki/Water "Water") [Water](/wiki/Water "Water") and [](/wiki/Crude_Oil "Crude Oil") [Crude Oil](/wiki/Crude_Oil "Crude Oil").  
A [](/wiki/Resource_Well_Pressurizer "Resource Well Pressurizer") [Resource Well Pressurizer](/wiki/Resource_Well_Pressurizer "Resource Well Pressurizer") can be built on the central node to pressurize (or activate) it, which allows [](/wiki/Resource_Well_Extractor "Resource Well Extractor") [Resource Well Extractors](/wiki/Resource_Well_Extractor "Resource Well Extractor") to be built on the secondary (satellite) nodes, allowing the fluid to be extracted. 

Resource Wells can be scanned using the [Resource Scanner](/wiki/Resource_Scanner "Resource Scanner"). It is not possible to scan for them separately, meaning scanning for Crude Oil points to both [Resource Nodes](/wiki/Resource_Node "Resource Node") and Wells.

## Purity

The purity of a satellite node affects how much fluid can be extracted from it. The Resource Well potential is the sum of how much fluid can be extracted from all satellite nodes in that particular Resource Well. [Overclocking](/wiki/Overclocking "Overclocking") the Pressurizer increases this sum and therefore affects all satellite nodes equally, e.g. overclocking it to 200% will cause all Extractors to extract twice as much fluid. 

Purity | m3/min at 100% | m3/min at 250%   
---|---|---  
Impure | 30 | 75   
Normal | 60 | 150   
Pure | 120 | 300

## Tips

  * Extracting Water from Resource Wells is usually less power-efficient than building [Water Extractors](/wiki/Water_Extractor "Water Extractor") on a water surface to produce the same amount of Water.
  * For Crude Oil extraction, it is also more efficient to extract using [Oil Extractors](/wiki/Oil_Extractor "Oil Extractor") on Crude Oil [nodes](/wiki/Node "Node"), unless all the nodes have been occupied.
