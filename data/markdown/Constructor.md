# Constructor

## Constructor

[ ](/wiki/File:Constructor.png "Constructor.png")

_Crafts 1 part into another part.  
Can be automated by feeding component parts in via a Conveyor Belt connected to the input port. The resulting parts can be automatically extracted by connecting a Conveyor Belt to the output port._

## Overclocking

_Main article:_[Clock speed](/wiki/Clock_speed "Clock speed")

The Constructor can be overclocked using [Power Shards](/wiki/Power_Shard "Power Shard"). Overclocking increases the input/output speed of the Constructor at the cost of exponentially increased [power](/wiki/Power "Power") demand. 

Clock speed | 25% | 50% | 75% | 100% | 150% | 200% | 250%   
---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---  
Consumption (MW)  | 0.64 | 1.6 | 2.73 | 4 | 6.84 | 10 | 13.43   
  
## Current issues

  * Since the release of [1.0](/wiki/1.0 "1.0"), Constructors have multiple visual bugs during animation, including the black box that used to be stamped into parts disappearing upon being stamped and the black box randomly appearing beside the press.

## Early game development

Prior to Closed Alpha, In early game development there was not a dedicated constructor, but that function was combined in a multi-purpose Manufacturing Plant. In Sprint 6 (week 12), the constructors became its own buildable.
