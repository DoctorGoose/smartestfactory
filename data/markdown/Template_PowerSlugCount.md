# Template:PowerSlugCount

[[purge](https://satisfactory.wiki.gg/wiki/Template:PowerSlugCount?action=purge)]

This template creates a summary table of [Power Slugs](/wiki/Power_Slug "Power Slug") and their respective number of [Power Shards](/wiki/Power_Shard "Power Shard"). 

Example: `{{PowerSlugCount|blue = 5|yellow = 3|purple = 2}}`

Slug Color | Blue | Yellow | Purple | Total   
---|---|---|---|---  
Slug Number | 5 | 3 | 2 | 10   
Power Shards | 3 | 4 | 5 | 12   
  
There are currently 10 Power Slugs known in-game; 5 blue, 3 yellow, and 2 purple. 1 slug of each type is consumed as part of [research](/wiki/Research "Research") in the [MAM](/wiki/MAM "MAM"). In addition, 1 [Power Shard](/wiki/Power_Shard "Power Shard") is consumed to unlock the [overclocking](/wiki/Overclocking "Overclocking") function. The upper limit of obtainable Power Shards is infinite, as they can be synthesized in a [Quantum Encoder](/wiki/Quantum_Encoder "Quantum Encoder") and [Lizard Doggos](/wiki/Lizard_Doggo "Lizard Doggo") can generate an infinite amount of slugs over time. The upper limit of non-renewable, usable Power Shards is 12.
