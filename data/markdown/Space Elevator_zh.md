**太空电梯** 是一座特殊的建筑，用于通过提供[项目组装部件](/wiki/Category:Project_Assembly_parts "Category:Project Assembly parts")来完成项目组装的各个阶段。前三次提交会解锁两个更高级别的[里程碑](/wiki/Milestones/zh?action=edit&redlink=1 "Milestones/zh \(page does not exist\)")层，第四次提交会解锁最后的级别9，第五次提交会启动项目组装，并在[AWESOME 商店](/wiki/AWESOME_Shop/zh?action=edit&redlink=1 "AWESOME Shop/zh \(page does not exist\)")解锁[“星球员工”杯](/wiki/%27Employee_of_the_Planet%27_Cup/zh?action=edit&redlink=1 "'Employee of the Planet' Cup/zh \(page does not exist\)")。 

从[世界](/wiki/World/zh "World/zh")的任何地方都能看到它，因此它是一个有效的导航地标。

## 用法

必须建造太空电梯才能解锁[级别3及以上](/wiki/Milestones/zh?action=edit&redlink=1 "Milestones/zh \(page does not exist\)")。游戏中同一时间只能建造一部太空电梯；不过，它可以随时拆卸和迁移，而不会损失进度。 

项目组装部件可以从库存[`Tab ↹`](/wiki/Controls/zh?action=edit&redlink=1 "Controls/zh \(page does not exist\)")中手动提供，也可以通过连接的传送带提供。虽然使用[建造枪](/wiki/Build_Gun/zh "Build Gun/zh")拆卸太空电梯会返回所有已装载的物品，但物品一旦装入电梯，就无法手动取回。 

装满所需物品后，用鼠标向下拖动手柄 2 次（装载→封存→发送）。发送后，将播放一段动画，货物平台将被弹射到太空中，沿着中央轨道滑行。一段时间后，它又会降落并停靠在默认位置。 

如果包裹已密封但未发送，而用户界面已关闭，则必须再次拉动手柄（加载 → 密封）。 

在多人游戏中，太空电梯及其进度由所有玩家共享。用户界面中的手柄不会在玩家之间同步。

### 项目组装启动（结局）

**展开以显示剧透**

完成项目组装的第五阶段就会启动结局，在结局中，必须通过太空电梯显示屏执行一系列特定任务。完成后，最后一次拉动控制杆，播放太空电梯的特殊动画，即可启动 “项目组装”。 

电梯会组装出一艘巨大的飞船，飞船会飞离并[爆破](/wiki/Ballistic_Warp_Drive/zh?action=edit&redlink=1 "Ballistic Warp Drive/zh \(page does not exist\)")打开一个虫洞，然后飞过虫洞。完成这一系列动作后，ADA 会再次说话，在字幕开始时向玩家表示祝贺。从这时起，电梯会重新定位，形成[FICSIT](/wiki/FICSIT/zh?action=edit&redlink=1 "FICSIT/zh \(page does not exist\)")标志。控制台将永久显示 "项目组装已启动。先驱者目的结束"。

## Tips

  * If you are placing a Space Elevator on a foundation platform, you can get centering guidelines by placing observation towers at two cardinal, non-aligned compass points (N/E, N/W, S/E, or S/W, but not N/S or E/W) on your platform, then stand on one of the towers, you will get alignment guidelines to place the center of the elevator in the center of the platform.
  * An easier alternative is to find the center of the platform, look directly down, place a hologram under your feet, lock it with [`H`](/wiki/Controls "Controls"), move away, confirm that the elevator is facing the right way and build it.
  * Due to the high cost of project parts, early-stage pioneers might want to prevent overproducing them, in order to save up on parts that are harder to manufacture. Later on, the project parts can be a good source of [AWESOME Sink](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") points. 
    * Many early parts are used to craft later parts, so extras can usually be saved to speed up production later on.
  * Strategic use of [Somersloops](/wiki/Somersloop "Somersloop") can be used to ease Project Part production - by amplifying the machine making each stage of Project Part, one Ballistic Warp Drive will only cost 1.25 Smart Plates, instead of the usual 5.
  * In multiplayer, only the pioneer who sends the parts will receive the relevant achievement, so multiple pioneers cannot both get every achievement in a single shared save.

## Trivia

  * After each delivery, the Space Elevator will ascend to the sky for a few minutes then descend back to the lower level.
  * Only the anchors of the Space Elevator are constructed by the pioneer, which explains its rather cheap price. When built, the anchor grabs the shaft, which is dropped from space.
  * The delivery mechanism of the Space Elevator does have collision maps, so if the player managed to work their way up to the top, they would be able to walk around on it. However, this collision area is very inaccurate, meaning that you won't be walking directly on the platform.
  * The Project Assembly Station can be seen in game, above the Space Elevator, and visually builds new components with each delivery.[3]
  * The elevator's shaft texture is very low-quality above the platform. Below the platform, the shaft has a much higher-quality texture. This part of the elevator has blinking lights that flash in an upward-traveling pattern.
  * It is possible to complete the last phase within 30 hours. In speedrun community, it is often referred as 'Npackage% speedrun' where N can be 1, 2, 3 or 4, and '1' is omitted for the first phase.
  * The internal name of the Space Elevator is "Spelevator".[4]
  * An early concept had considered using the Space Elevator for [sinking items](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink"), but this was discarded due to concerns about getting all the resources to one central location.[5]
  * Despite having 6 conveyor inputs, none of the phases require more than 4 different inputs. No other building in the game has such redundant inputs that no recipe would fully utilize.



### Food Court

  * According to [Jace](/wiki/Jace "Jace"), [Sofi](/wiki/Sofi "Sofi"), and [Snutt](/wiki/Snutt "Snutt"), there is supposedly a food court at the top of the Space Elevator.[6][7]
    * According to [Snutt](/wiki/Snutt "Snutt"), Project Assembly both _is_ a Food Court and _has_ a Food Court.[8]



#### Menu

  * If the planet's [flora](/wiki/Category:Flora "Category:Flora") & [fauna](/wiki/Category:Fauna "Category:Fauna") are to be used in preparing food to be served in the food court, it is possible that the digestive tracts of the native species are either non-existent or toxic to the intended diners as the FICSIT catering department will likely need to order synthetic sausage casings.[9]
  * [Lizard Doggo](/wiki/Lizard_Doggo "Lizard Doggo") burgers, tacos, kebabs, stir-fry, and gyoza will be on the menu.[10]
    * The [Lizard Doggo](/wiki/Lizard_Doggo "Lizard Doggo") burger will cost [1 coupon](/wiki/FICSIT_Coupon "FICSIT Coupon").[11]
    * Vegetarian options have been considered but not confirmed.[12]
  * [Hog](/wiki/Fluffy-tailed_Hog "Fluffy-tailed Hog") steak will be a special on the menu.[13]
