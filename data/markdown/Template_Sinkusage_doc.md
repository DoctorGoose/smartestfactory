The Sinkusage template is used in the Usage section of parts under AWESOME Sink. It displays whether the part can be sunk into the AWESOME Sink and if it can, how many points it yields. 

## Usage

  * 1 – className of the target item – **mandatory**



## Examples

`{{[Sinkusage](/wiki/Template:Sinkusage "Template:Sinkusage")|Desc_Cable_C}}`

**[](/wiki/Cable "Cable")[Cable](/wiki/Cable "Cable")** can be sunk into the [](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") [AWESOME Sink](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") to yield **24 points**.

**[](/wiki/Plutonium_Fuel_Rod "Plutonium Fuel Rod")[Plutonium Fuel Rod](/wiki/Plutonium_Fuel_Rod "Plutonium Fuel Rod")** can be sunk into the [](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") [AWESOME Sink](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") to yield **153,184 points**.
