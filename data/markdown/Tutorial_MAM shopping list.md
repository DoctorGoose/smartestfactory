This shopping list of items is designed to answer the question: At each [Milestone](/wiki/Milestones "Milestones"), what do I need to collect to unlock as much as possible in the [MAM](/wiki/MAM "MAM")? 

  * Under each research node is the items required and the reward for unlocking if the node isn't its own reward e.g. [](/wiki/Rebar_Gun "Rebar Gun") [The Rebar Gun](/wiki/Rebar_Gun "Rebar Gun"). 
    * If 100 or more of a required part needs to be found at crash sites, or a descendent node, they are listed below the line and are separate from the total to find e.g. [](/wiki/SAM_Fluctuator "SAM Fluctuator") [SAM Fluctuator](/wiki/SAM_Fluctuator "SAM Fluctuator")
  * Some [Alternate Recipes](/wiki/MAM#Alternate_Recipes "MAM") require MAM nodes or additional milestones to be unlocked, these are listed under the alternate recipe e.g. [](/wiki/File:Wire.png) [Caterium Wire](/wiki/Wire#Alternate_recipes_analysis "Wire")
  * Alternate recipes and research nodes are listed at the earliest possible stage they can be unlocked, not when they can be crafted. These have a reference saying why, and their shopping list needs are listed separately. 
    * If any of the parts required **to craft them** need to be found at crash sites, they are _italicized_ e.g. _[](/wiki/Blade_Runners "Blade Runners")[Blade Runners](/wiki/Blade_Runners "Blade Runners")_ [1]
    * If any of the parts required **to craft them** are only available at higher Tiers, they are ~~crossed out~~ e.g.
  * Anything unlocked at an earlier milestone that can be crafted is not listed. E.g. after **Tier 1 - Field Research** , it is assumed you have a ready supply of [](/wiki/Quartz_Crystal "Quartz Crystal") [Quartz Crystals](/wiki/Quartz_Crystal "Quartz Crystal").
  * Items that are required to obtain [](/wiki/Hard_Drive "Hard Drive") [Hard Drives](/wiki/Hard_Drive "Hard Drive") from [Crash Sites](/wiki/Crash_Site "Crash Site") are not factored into the sums listed here.
  * No guarantee is given that there are sufficient quantities of items around crash sites.
