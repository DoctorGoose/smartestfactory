# Patch 0.1

Patch Notes: Early Access – **Early Access Release** – Build 95718. This patch was released on 19 March 2019. It is the first publicly accessible build of the game (excluding the [Satisfactory Test Weekend](/wiki/Alpha_Test_Weekend "Alpha Test Weekend")). 

The original post can be viewed on Satisfactory's [official website](http://web.archive.org/web/20190430175151/https://www.satisfactorygame.com/patch-notes), as well as [Discord](https://discord.com/channels/370472939054956546/557603412565426196/557611533341949966) and [Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/SatisfactoryGame/comments/b306cs).

## Intro

THIS IS HAPPENING! The game is now released and we are incredibly excited for you all to play Satisfactory. Here are some patch notes detailing the changes that went in since the Alpha Test Weekend. There is lots of new content in the Early Access version and there are likely more tweaks and fixes in this version than these patch notes cover. 

Hope you all will be having an amazing time with the game! 

## Features

  * Unlocked [Tiers](/wiki/Milestones "Milestones") 4 - 6
  * Unlocked the [Caterium](/wiki/Caterium_Ore "Caterium Ore") Research Chain
  * Added work-in-progress localisation to a number of languages
  * Emotes ;)



## Balancing

  * Rebalanced Steel: Steel Plate and Reinforced Steel Plate are now [Steel Beam](/wiki/Steel_Beam "Steel Beam") and [Encased Industrial Beam](/wiki/Encased_Industrial_Beam "Encased Industrial Beam") (both have a different cost than before, Encased Industrial Beam requiring [Concrete](/wiki/Concrete "Concrete") to produce)
  * Rebalanced [Heavy Modular Frame](/wiki/Heavy_Modular_Frame "Heavy Modular Frame")
  * Rebalanced costs of Tiers 2 - 5
  * Rebalanced costs of Caterium Milestones
  * Moved Caterium research from Tiers 4 and 5 to Tiers 5 and 6
  * Moved [Object Scanner](/wiki/Object_Scanner "Object Scanner") and [Beacon](/wiki/Beacon "Beacon") to [Hub](/wiki/The_HUB "The HUB") Upgrade 5
  * Moved [Conveyor Belt Mk.3](/wiki/Conveyor_Belt "Conveyor Belt") and [Storage Mk.2](/wiki/Storage_Container_Mk.2 "Storage Container Mk.2") from Tier 4 to Tier 5
  * Tweaked Blade Runners: Sprint speed boost down to 50% from 75% and reduced the fall damage curve slightly
  * Locked the [Space Elevator](/wiki/Space_Elevator "Space Elevator") Phase that opens up Tiers 7 and 8
  * Tweaked Onboarding hints a little bit
  * [Xeno-Zapper](/wiki/Xeno-Zapper "Xeno-Zapper") damage increased by 25%
  * [Xeno-Basher](/wiki/Xeno-Basher "Xeno-Basher") damage increased by 40% and nerfed knockback and attack speed slightly
  * Updated [Somersloop](/wiki/Somersloop "Somersloop") and [Mercer Sphere](/wiki/Mercer_Sphere "Mercer Sphere") description names to convey they are work in progress and currently net no rewards
  * Excluded research for Somersloop and Mercer Sphere
  * Adjusted a few alternate recipes unlocked via the [Hard Drive](/wiki/Hard_Drive "Hard Drive") research
  * Smaller tutorial flow fixes
  * Players don't respawn with full health anymore
  * [Assembler](/wiki/Assembler "Assembler") now costs [Modular Frames](/wiki/Modular_Frame "Modular Frame") instead of [Reinforced Iron Plates](/wiki/Reinforced_Iron_Plate "Reinforced Iron Plate")
  * Changed the default chat key from T to Enter

  * Fixed desync issues in multiplayer when dismantling wires connected to poles
  * Fixed desync issues on [landing pads](/wiki/U-Jelly_Landing_Pad "U-Jelly Landing Pad") that caused stutters
  * Fixed issues with escape/main menu on different screen ratios
  * Fixed FOV issues with edibles & intro sequence
  * Fixed issue with [Chainsaw](/wiki/Chainsaw "Chainsaw") normals

  * Fixed that non-solid resources were hand-mineable
  * Several crash fixes
  * Fixed creatures making sounds after they die

  * Fixed several spelling mistakes

  * Fixed [Craft Bench](/wiki/Craft_Bench "Craft Bench") / [Equipment Workshop](/wiki/Equipment_Workshop "Equipment Workshop") not being marked as occupied for clients when other players are using them
  * Fixed material bugs when dismantling [Conveyor Belts](/wiki/Conveyor_Belt "Conveyor Belt")
  * Gas damage is now applied to players in vehicles
  * Fall damage is now applied to players in vehicles
  * Fixed that players see their own player marker on Compass after respawning
  * Fixed Dismantle Highlight and Collision bug
  * Fixed that the mining animation doesn't continue playing when the player runs out of inventory space
  * Fixed the sound bug on the Biomass Burners integrated into the Hub
  * Fixed that jumping during the end animation of the Intro Sequence send the player flying back into space
  * Fixed that entering water during Onboarding resets the key restrictions
  * Fixed Beacon names not appearing on the Compass
  * Lots of level bug fixes across the world
  * Several subtitle, VO and spelling fixes
  * Fixed holding space bar and Alt+Tab out of the game while crafting
  * Fixed that Shift+Click sometimes needs to be pressed twice to function
  * Fixed players not requipping their gear when they are revived
  * Fixed FOV not affecting equipment VFX
  * Fixed that manually inputting numbers for the To-Do list in the Build Menu overwrites the players selected hotkeys
  * Fixed "Power Consumption" and "Power Production" being switched in the Generator and Manufacturing menus
  * Fixed client health not regenerating
