# Recipes

**Recipes** are conversions from a set of resources (ingredients) to another set of resources (products). There are three types of recipes: 

  * Crafting recipes – Used to craft [items](/wiki/Items "Items") and [fluids](/wiki/Fluids "Fluids") in factory buildings, the [Craft Bench](/wiki/Craft_Bench "Craft Bench"), or [Equipment Workshop](/wiki/Equipment_Workshop "Equipment Workshop"). They are non-reversible; once a resource is crafted, it cannot be deconstructed back. However, packaging and unpackaging fluids in the [Packager](/wiki/Packager "Packager") is reversible via mirrored recipes.
  * Building recipes – Used to construct [buildings](/wiki/Building "Building") with the [Build Gun](/wiki/Build_Gun "Build Gun"). Buildings can be dismantled at no cost, returning all resources used to construct them, including their contents (if any).
  * Customizer recipes – Used to convert customizable buildings to another variant using the [Customizer](/wiki/Customizer "Customizer"). Similarly to building recipes, all costs are refundable.



New recipes are unlocked from [Milestones](/wiki/Milestones "Milestones"), [Research](/wiki/Research "Research"), the [AWESOME Shop](/wiki/AWESOME_Shop "AWESOME Shop") and [AWESOME Sink](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink"). [Alternate recipes](/wiki/Alternate_recipes "Alternate recipes") are unlocked by scanning lost [Hard Drives](/wiki/Hard_Drive "Hard Drive") found in [Crash Sites](/wiki/Crash_Site "Crash Site"). Some recipes are only available during seasonal events, such as [FICSMAS](/wiki/FICSMAS "FICSMAS").
