# Foundry

## Foundry

[ ](/wiki/File:Foundry.png "Foundry.png")

_Smelts 2 resources into alloy ingots.  
Can be automated by feeding ore in via Conveyor Belts connected to the input ports. The resulting ingots can be automatically extracted by connecting a Conveyor Belt to the output port._

The **Foundry** is a [building](/wiki/Building "Building") that smelts two ore resources into alloy ingots. It cannot be used for smelting single ore resources into single ingot resources nor does it behave like a Mk.2 [Smelter](/wiki/Smelter "Smelter").

## Overclocking

_Main article:_[Clock speed](/wiki/Clock_speed "Clock speed")

The Foundry can be overclocked using [Power Shards](/wiki/Power_Shard "Power Shard"). Overclocking increases the input/output speed of the Foundry at the cost of exponentially increased [power](/wiki/Power "Power") demand. 

Clock speed | 25% | 50% | 75% | 100% | 150% | 200% | 250%   
---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---  
Consumption (MW)  | 2.56 | 6.4 | 10.94 | 16 | 27.35 | 40 | 53.72

  * The [Indicator Light](/wiki/Indicator_Light "Indicator Light") of the Foundry appears purple in its UI icon.

## Early game development

Prior to Closed Alpha, in Sprint 20 (week 40) there was a Mk.2 Foundry that was an improved version of the Mk.2 Smelter. Both Mk.2 variants were removed in Closed Alpha.
