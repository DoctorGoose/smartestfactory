## Assembler  
  
[ ](/wiki/File:Assembler.png "Assembler.png")

_Crafts 2 parts into another part.  
Can be automated by feeding component parts in via Conveyor Belts connected to the input ports. The resulting parts can be automatically extracted by connecting a Conveyor Belt to the output port._

## 超頻

_Main article:_[Clock speed](/wiki/Clock_speed "Clock speed")

The Assembler/zh can be overclocked using [Power Shards](/wiki/Power_Shard "Power Shard"). Overclocking increases the input/output speed of the Assembler/zh at the cost of exponentially increased [power](/wiki/Power "Power") demand.
