The **Converter** is a late-game [building](/wiki/Building "Building") used to convert [ores](/wiki/Category:Ores "Category:Ores") into each other at the cost of [](/wiki/Reanimated_SAM "Reanimated SAM") [Reanimated SAM](/wiki/Reanimated_SAM "Reanimated SAM"), as well as produce [](/wiki/Ficsite_Ingot "Ficsite Ingot") [Ficsite Ingots](/wiki/Ficsite_Ingot "Ficsite Ingot"), [](/wiki/Excited_Photonic_Matter "Excited Photonic Matter") [Excited Photonic Matter](/wiki/Excited_Photonic_Matter "Excited Photonic Matter") and [](/wiki/Dark_Matter_Residue "Dark Matter Residue") [Dark Matter Residue](/wiki/Dark_Matter_Residue "Dark Matter Residue"). 

It has a fluctuating power demand, akin to [Particle Accelerators](/wiki/Particle_Accelerator "Particle Accelerator").

## Overclocking

_Main article:_[Clock speed](/wiki/Clock_speed "Clock speed")

The Converter can be overclocked using [Power Shards](/wiki/Power_Shard "Power Shard"). Overclocking increases the input/output speed of the Converter at the cost of exponentially increased [power](/wiki/Power "Power") demand. 

## Power fluctuation

Like the [Particle Accelerator](/wiki/Particle_Accelerator "Particle Accelerator"), the Converter has oscillating power usage. The power curve roughly resembles a triangle wave, starting at 62.5% max power and ending back at 62.5% after hitting 100% and 25% during the production cycle. Generally, minimum power usage is 100 MW while the max is 400 MW - on average it is 250 MW. 

## Resource transmutation

Many Converter recipes transmute one raw resource into another. This allows you to exceed the limited extraction amount of any raw resource (other than Crude Oil and SAM) and use rare resources in factories without setting up the logistics to transport them across the world. As all these recipes have the same SAM and power requirements per building, the most SAM efficient, most power efficient, and most space efficient recipes will always be one and the same. 

### Multi-step transmutation

Raw resources can be sent through a series of Converters to be transformed into resources with no direct Converter recipe. To find a chain of recipes to convert one resource into another, follow the arrows on the diagram. Generally, it is more SAM and power-efficient to go down the diagram rather than up it, and the most efficient paths down the diagram are Iron Ore → Sulfur → Copper Ore → Bauxite → Nitrogen/Uranium and Limestone → Coal → Raw Quartz → Caterium Ore → Nitrogen/Bauxite → Uranium. 

### Raw resource generation

A closed loop of Converters can be used to manufacture raw resources using only power and SAM. Without using Somersloops, the most efficient loop consists of Converters set to the Coal (Limestone), Sulfur (Coal), and Limestone (Sulfur) recipes. A full cycle around this loop creates 20% excess raw material, which can be taken out of the loop and transmuted into any raw resource (other than Crude Oil and SAM).

#### Generating Sulfur

With no Somersloops, the conversion rate of the Limestone -> Coal -> Sulfur loop is 23 Reanimated SAM to 6 Sulfur. However, with Somersloops, it becomes advantageous to change the cycle by adding an Iron Ore step between Limestone and Coal. Thus the cycle becomes Sulfur to Limestone to Iron Ore to Coal to Sulfur. This gives us a 4th slot for Somersloops, making the final conversion ratio 39.33333 Reanimated SAM to 364 Sulfur with Somersloops in all 4 Converters. This means that a pure SAM node at 250% will produce 5,552 Sulfur/min if the Reanimated SAM constructors are slooped as well. This is roughly half the combined output of every Sulfur node on the map at 250%, which is 10,800. Note that without Somersloops, adding Iron Ore to the cycle results in using more SAM for no additional gain (or loss) of output. Also note that to Somersloop the 4 Reanimated SAM Constructors plus all 242 Converters required to produce the maximum volume of Sulfur from a pure SAM node requires 488 Somersloops, well more than the 106 Somersloops in the game.

  * [Patch 1.0.0.4](/wiki/Patch_1.0.0.4 "Patch 1.0.0.4"): Fixed sounds for the [Oil Refinery](/wiki/Refinery "Refinery"), [Assembler](/wiki/Assembler "Assembler"), Converter and [Manufacturer](/wiki/Manufacturer "Manufacturer") sometimes stacking and playing multiple times
  * [Patch 1.0](/wiki/Patch_1.0 "Patch 1.0")
    * Reintroduced with a new mesh.
    * This building utilizes Strange Alien Matter, aka S.A.M., to enable matter conversion and transmutation. It can be used to convert one raw resource into another, for example Limestone into Iron and Copper into Caterium, allowing players to put resource nodes to good use that might have been neglected otherwise.
    * It is also used for various processes essential for the development of other Tier 9 technology, such as the production of the synthetic metal Ficsite and the generation of Excited Photonic Matter.
