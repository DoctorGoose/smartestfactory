[ ](/wiki/File:Iron_Plate.png "Iron Plate.png")

### Unlocked by

## Item

### Stack size

200

### [Sink points](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink")

6

**Iron Plate** is one of the first two resources available to craft after [The HUB](/wiki/The_HUB "The HUB") is built, the other being [Iron Rod](/wiki/Iron_Rod "Iron Rod"). Iron Plates are one of the basic building materials and are used for many structures and recipes.

### AWESOME Sink

**[](/wiki/Iron_Plate "Iron Plate")Iron Plate** can be sunk into the [](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") [AWESOME Sink](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") to yield **6 points**.

## Tips

  * The fastest way to make Iron Plates from Iron Ingots is using the Solid Steel and Steel Coated Plate alternate recipes. This, however, involves using Coal and Crude Oil, which are rarer resources.



## Trivia

  * Iron Plates are the only pure-Iron component that lacks a pure-Steel alternate recipe. If not for Iron Plates, Iron Ingots could be completely eliminated from production except as a component of Steel Ingots.
