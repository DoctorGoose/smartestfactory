# Alclad Aluminum Sheet

## Alclad Aluminum Sheet

[ ](/wiki/File:Alclad_Aluminum_Sheet.png "Alclad Aluminum Sheet.png")

_Used to make products that require high heat conduction or a high specific strength. Thin, lightweight, and highly durable._

### Unlocked by

[Tier 7](/wiki/Tier_7 "Tier 7") \- Bauxite Refinement

### Class name

Desc_AluminumPlate_C

## Item

### Stack size

200

### [Sink points](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink")

266

**Alclad Aluminum Sheet** is a late-game component used for building [Mk.5 Conveyor Belts](/wiki/Conveyor_Belt "Conveyor Belt") and [Lifts](/wiki/Conveyor_Lift "Conveyor Lift") among other things.
