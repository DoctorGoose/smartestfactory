# Silica

## Silica

[ ](/wiki/File:Silica.png "Silica.png")

_Derived from Raw Quartz. Commonly used to create glass structures, in advanced refinement processes, and for alternative production of electronics._

### Unlocked by

[MAM Quartz Research](/wiki/Quartz_Research "Quartz Research") \- Silica OR  
[Tier 7](/wiki/Tier_7 "Tier 7") \- Bauxite Refinement OR  
[Tier 7](/wiki/Tier_7 "Tier 7") \- Control System Development

### Class name

Desc_Silica_C

## Item

### Stack size

200

### [Sink points](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink")

20

### Abbrev­iation

Silica

**Silica** is an intermediate item used for a variety of products. It is produced from [](/wiki/Raw_Quartz "Raw Quartz") [Raw Quartz](/wiki/Raw_Quartz "Raw Quartz"), as a byproduct of [](/wiki/Alumina_Solution "Alumina Solution") [Alumina Solution](/wiki/Alumina_Solution "Alumina Solution") production or gathered in small amounts from cutting down coral.

## History

  * [Patch 1.0](/wiki/Patch_1.0 "Patch 1.0"): 
    * Introduced Distilled Silica recipe
    * Cheap Silica 
      * Changed recipe craft time from 16 to 8 seconds
  * [Patch 0.5.0.8](/wiki/Patch_0.5.0.8 "Patch 0.5.0.8"): Increased Silica max stack size from 100 to 200, Updated Silica description.
  * [Patch 0.5.0.0](/wiki/Patch_0.5.0.0 "Patch 0.5.0.0")
    * [AWESOME Shop](/wiki/AWESOME_Shop "AWESOME Shop") Schematics now use dependencies, which means that they are not available for purchase until the player has unlocked the right ingredients 
      * This applies for a bunch of items, such as [Signs](/wiki/Signs "Signs"), [Lights](/wiki/Lights "Lights") and many aesthetic build pieces and Materials that use more advanced resources, the common ones being [Quartz Crystals](/wiki/Quartz_Crystal "Quartz Crystal"), [Quickwire](/wiki/Quickwire "Quickwire"), Silica and [Steel Beams](/wiki/Steel_Beam "Steel Beam")
  * [Patch 0.3.3.2](/wiki/Patch_0.3.3.2 "Patch 0.3.3.2"): New icons for changed resources: Silica
  * [Patch 0.3.3.0](/wiki/Patch_0.3.3.0 "Patch 0.3.3.0"): Reworked & optimized several resources/parts: Silica
  * [Patch 0.1.16](/wiki/Patch_0.1.16 "Patch 0.1.16"): 
    * Added the mesh and icon for Silica
    * Introduced standard recipe (previously only available via alternate recipes).
