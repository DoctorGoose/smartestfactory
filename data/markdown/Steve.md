# Steve

| **[Removed content](/wiki/Category:Removed_content "Category:Removed content")** This article or section is about a feature that has been removed from the game.   
---|---  
  
Caterina as well as Steve have been officially retconned from Satisfactory's story and universe as of 16th of August 2024.[1]

[](/wiki/File:Steve.png)

[](/wiki/File:Steve.png "Enlarge")

Steve

**Steve** was to be an assistant to [Caterina Parks](/wiki/Caterina_Parks "Caterina Parks"), an employee at [FICSIT Inc.](/wiki/FICSIT_Inc. "FICSIT Inc.") and the creator of the 'Introduction' on the [original website](https://web.archive.org/web/20181206044653/https://www.satisfactorygame.com/) describing some duties to the [pioneer](/wiki/Pioneer "Pioneer"). He has been described as ambitious and very stressed.[2]

## Former Speculation

  * Possibly a primary point of contact for the pioneer during their time working for [FICSIT Inc.](/wiki/FICSIT_Inc. "FICSIT Inc.")
  * The male voice in the game file _0379.wav_ saying _"Huh, must have been a dream."_ possibly belonged to him. The sound is currently unused in the game, as Steve is no longer part of Satisfactory's cast or lore. It should be noted that a female voice (possibly Caterina's) said the exact same thing in the file _0926.wav_.
