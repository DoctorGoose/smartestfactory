## Costruzione

Beams can be built one at a time or 10 at once using the [Zoop](/wiki/Build_Gun#Zoop "Build Gun") construction mode. 

The rotation of beams can be adjusted by scrolling [](/wiki/File:Keyboard_White_Mouse_Middle.png "Middle") before placing them. They also have a customizable length. Their build cost increases by 2× [](/wiki/Steel_Beam "Steel Beam") [Steel Beam](/wiki/Steel_Beam "Steel Beam") for every additional 4 meters, and can be extended up to 40 meters (for example, a beam 5 meters long will cost 4 [](/wiki/Steel_Beam "Steel Beam") [Steel Beams](/wiki/Steel_Beam "Steel Beam")). Beams, their supports and connectors all have soft clearance. 

Beams can snap to other beams, allowing continuous chains of beams to be built. [Walls](/wiki/Walls "Walls") and Architectural structures can be built onto beams. 

The color of [](/wiki/Painted_Beam "Painted Beam") [Painted Beams](/wiki/Painted_Beam "Painted Beam") can be customized using the [Customizer](/wiki/Customizer "Customizer"). The primary swatch color will be used. [](/wiki/Metal_Beam "Metal Beam") [Metal Beams](/wiki/Metal_Beam "Metal Beam") cannot be customized this way. 

### Tips

  * While beams can only be built to 40m maximum length they can be directed by aiming at snapping points much further away
  * The middle of [foundations ](/wiki/Foundations "Foundations") can be snapped to the end of beams
  * [Signs ](/wiki/Signs "Signs") snap to one side of a beam, that side rotates with the beam allowing alignment of signs with surfaces otherwise not possible
