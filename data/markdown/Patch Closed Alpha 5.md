## Intro

With the upcoming Early Access release we want to test Tier 6 with you all! So, in this update we have unlocked Tier 6 and the Caterium Research Chain. To start the latter, just find some Caterium Ore and analyse it in the MAM. Of course all the polish and Quality of Life fixes for the Alpha Test Weekend have also made it into this build, together with several fixes for bugs that we gathered over the weekend. The patch notes don't quite include everything we have done to improve the game, but should give a good summary! 

Hope you enjoy the patch, and give us your feedback ASAP so we can still improve upon before next week ðŸ˜ƒ <3 

## Features

  * Unlocked [Tier 6](/wiki/Tier_6 "Tier 6")
  * Unlocked the [Caterium Research](/wiki/Caterium_Research "Caterium Research") Chain



## Balancing

  * [Xeno-Zapper](/wiki/Xeno-Zapper "Xeno-Zapper") damage increased by 25%
  * [Xeno-Basher](/wiki/Xeno-Basher "Xeno-Basher") damage increased by 40%
  * Updated [Somersloop](/wiki/Somersloop "Somersloop") and [Mercer Sphere](/wiki/Mercer_Sphere "Mercer Sphere") description names to convey they are work in progress and currently net no rewards
  * Excluded research for Somersloop and Mercer Sphere
  * Adjusted a few alternate recipes unlocked via the [Hard Drive](/wiki/Hard_Drive "Hard Drive") research
  * Smaller [tutorial](/wiki/Tutorial "Tutorial") flow fixes
  * Players don't respawn with full [health](/wiki/Health "Health") anymore
  * [Assembler](/wiki/Assembler "Assembler") now costs Modular Frames instead of Reinforced Iron Plates
  * Changed the default chat key from T to Enter



## Bug fixes

  * Fixed desync issues in [multiplayer](/wiki/Multiplayer "Multiplayer") when dismantling [wires](/wiki/Power_Line "Power Line") connected to poles
  * Fixed desync issues on [landing pads](/wiki/U-Jelly_Landing_Pad "U-Jelly Landing Pad") that caused stutters
  * Fixed issues with escape/main menu on different screen ratios
  * Fixed FOV issues with edibles & intro sequence
  * Fixed issue with [Chainsaw](/wiki/Chainsaw "Chainsaw") normals
  * Fixed vehicles getting stuck in the [Truck Station](/wiki/Truck_Station "Truck Station")
  * Fixed that non-solid resources were hand-mineable
  * Several crash fixes
  * Fixed [creatures](/wiki/Category:Fauna "Category:Fauna") making sounds after they die
  * Fixed remaining focus issues
  * Fixed buildings having no legs on client
  * Fixed several spelling mistakes



## Buildings

  * Changed screen textures on [the Hub](/wiki/The_Hub "The Hub") & [Space Elevator](/wiki/Space_Elevator "Space Elevator")
  * Custom build sound and animation sounds for the Space Elevator



## UI

  * Increased item drop area in the Hub Milestone Menu
  * More icon updates to buildings, resources & equipment
  * Added damage direction indicator
  * Tweaked take damage post process effect
  * [Portable Miner](/wiki/Portable_Miner "Portable Miner") now has TWO buttons. Crazy.



## World

  * Green Kapok tree collision improvements
  * Full environment pass on Blue Crater (oil area east of Grassfields)
  * Gameplay pass on Blue Crater
  * Increased culling distance for creatures
  * Added last LODs for boulders in Grassfields and tweaked their culling distance
  * Updated pebble visuals
  * Updated [caves](/wiki/Cave "Cave") across the map
  * Added perimeter gas VFX for gas pillars
  * Ambient sound polish on world
  * Added a bunch of new foliage pick-up sounds
  * Started on visual improvements for cliffs (partial update)
  * Added sounds for resource [deposits](/wiki/Deposit "Deposit") being destroyed



## Wildlife

  * Increased culling distance for wildlife
  * Added bouncy functionality for the [Space Giraffe-Tick-Penguin-Whale Thing](/wiki/Space_Giraffe-Tick-Penguin-Whale_Thing "Space Giraffe-Tick-Penguin-Whale Thing")
  * Adjustment to small [Spitter](/wiki/Spitter "Spitter") aggro behaviour and combat
  * Adjustment to [Hog](/wiki/Hog "Hog") behaviour
  * Tweaked step height for [Spitters](/wiki/Spitter "Spitter"), [Hogs](/wiki/Hog "Hog") & [Space Giraffe](/wiki/Land_Whale "Land Whale")
  * Added collision to the [Giant Flying Manta](/wiki/Manta "Manta")
  * Improved [Stinger](/wiki/Stinger "Stinger") (spider-like enemy) movement and small Stinger attacks



## Vehicles

  * Increased simulation distance for [vehicles](/wiki/Vehicles "Vehicles")
  * Added honk functionality to [Tractor](/wiki/Tractor "Tractor")



## Equipment

  * Fixed dropped equipment meshes for [Chainsaw](/wiki/Chainsaw "Chainsaw") & [Rebar Gun](/wiki/Rebar_Gun "Rebar Gun")
  * [Rebar Gun](/wiki/Rebar_Gun "Rebar Gun") projectiles now ragdoll and launch players
  * Added ability to hit players with the [Xeno-Zapper](/wiki/Xeno-Zapper "Xeno-Zapper") etc.
  * Sound fixes to Xeno-Zapper and Basher
  * Added [flashlight](/wiki/Flashlight "Flashlight") sounds
