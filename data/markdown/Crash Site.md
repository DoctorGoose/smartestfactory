**Crash Sites** are crashed [FICSIT Freighters](/wiki/FICSIT_Freighter "FICSIT Freighter") that can be found in the [world](/wiki/World "World"), carrying [](/wiki/Hard_Drive "Hard Drive") [Hard Drives](/wiki/Hard_Drive "Hard Drive"). There are 118 Crash Sites in total. Crash Sites consist of: 

  * Freighter Drop-pod, containing the Hard Drive
  * Scattered components on the ground, which can be picked up (as listed below)
  * Other scattered wreckage

## Locating

  * Upon [researching](/wiki/Research "Research") ['Radio Signal Scanning'](/wiki/Quartz_Research "Quartz Research"), Crash Sites can be scanned for with an [Object Scanner](/wiki/Object_Scanner "Object Scanner").
  * Players can refer to [online maps](/wiki/Online_tools#save_editors "Online tools") for Crash Site locations and the requirements to unlock them. Take note of their z-position which represents the elevation (units in centimeters), and plan ahead.



## Retrieving the hard drive

### Unlock requirements

Unlocking a majority of pods will often require a certain amount of a part or power supplied. If those requirements are fulfilled, the handle in the UI becomes functional, allowing to open the hatch and retrieve the Hard Drive within. 

  * For those requiring a certain part, the part will be automatically inserted into the slot in the pod. The part cannot be retrieved once the hatch is opened. 
    * Occasionally, the required part can be found scattered around the Crash Site
  * For those requiring a certain amount of [power](/wiki/Power "Power") to be supplied, [Biomass Burners](/wiki/Biomass_Burner "Biomass Burner") can be utilized to power the pod temporarily. Most require less than 100MW, but only seven exceed 100MW and the max is 420MW. 
    * If connecting the pod to the main [power grid](/wiki/Power_grid "Power grid"), make sure to disconnect it once the drive is collected, as they will continue to draw power even after the hard drive has been retrieved.



Some pods have no unlock requirement, the handle is always functional and the drive can be retrieved at no cost.

## Tips

  * It is advised to keep building materials for [Biomass Burners](/wiki/Biomass_Burner "Biomass Burner") (or an [Alien Power Augmenter](/wiki/Alien_Power_Augmenter "Alien Power Augmenter")) for [Hard Drive](/wiki/Hard_Drive "Hard Drive") hunting. Using other types of generators will require fluid input, which may be complicated to setup for such temporary works. If a large amount of power is required, consider hooking it into the main power grid.
  * The most common items needed to open the pods are [Rotors](/wiki/Rotor "Rotor") and [Circuit Boards](/wiki/Circuit_Board "Circuit Board"), but in some cases they require [Solid Biofuel](/wiki/Solid_Biofuel "Solid Biofuel"), [Motors](/wiki/Motor "Motor"), [Stators](/wiki/Stator "Stator"), [Encased Industrial Beams](/wiki/Encased_Industrial_Beam "Encased Industrial Beam"), [High-Speed Connectors](/wiki/High-Speed_Connector "High-Speed Connector"), [Modular Frames](/wiki/Modular_Frame "Modular Frame"), [Crystal Oscillator](/wiki/Crystal_Oscillator "Crystal Oscillator"), [AI Limiters](/wiki/AI_Limiter "AI Limiter"), [Heat Sinks](/wiki/Heat_Sink "Heat Sink"), [Radio Control Units](/wiki/Radio_Control_Unit "Radio Control Unit"), [Turbo Motors](/wiki/Turbo_Motor "Turbo Motor"), [Black Powder](/wiki/Black_Powder "Black Powder"), [Steel Pipes](/wiki/Steel_Pipe "Steel Pipe") and [Supercomputers](/wiki/Supercomputer "Supercomputer")
  * It is advised to bring plenty of ammo for the [Rifle](/wiki/Rifle "Rifle"), [Fuel](/wiki/Jetpack#Fuel "Jetpack") for the [Jetpack](/wiki/Jetpack "Jetpack"), and a [Gas Mask](/wiki/Gas_Mask "Gas Mask"). A [Hazmat Suit](/wiki/Hazmat_Suit "Hazmat Suit") may also be useful.
  * The scattered resources can be used to open the Freighter Pod. You can often find a resource from one crash site that opens another, or even find the required resource at a drop site.
  * Once the [Hard Drive](/wiki/Hard_Drive "Hard Drive") has been retrieved, the Drop-pod and other scattered wreckage can be [dismantled](/wiki/Build_Gun#Dismantle_mode "Build Gun"), as to not venture out to the same site again and clear the area. There are 6 wreckage pieces around every site (including the empty pod); each wreckage piece will always give some number of three of the following item types: Iron Plates, Iron Rods, Screws, Wire, Cable, Copper Sheets, Reinforced Iron Plates, Rotors, Stators, or rarely even Motors or Modular Frames.

## History

  * [Patch 1.1.0.2](/wiki/Patch_1.1.0.2 "Patch 1.1.0.2"): Fixed Crash Sites respawning after being dismantled
  * [Patch 1.1.0.0](/wiki/Patch_1.1.0.0 "Patch 1.1.0.0")
    * Introduced Crash Site Dismantling 
      * You will also now will finally be able to clean up debris from Crash Sites from your biomes and get some extra resources for your efforts by doing so
      * Just remember that you must open and retrieve the [Hard Drive](/wiki/Hard_Drive "Hard Drive") inside the Crash Site first to be able to dismantle everything
  * [Patch 1.0.0.4](/wiki/Patch_1.0.0.4 "Patch 1.0.0.4"): Fixed two Crash Site items that showed the incorrect item, they now show Silica as they should
  * [Patch 0.8.2.7](/wiki/Patch_0.8.2.7 "Patch 0.8.2.7"): Potential fix to a bug that made most pickupables/interactables respawn after reloading a save
  * [Patch 0.8.0.5](/wiki/Patch_0.8.0.5 "Patch 0.8.0.5"): Fixed crash when opening an empty Crash Site
  * [Patch 0.8.0.0](/wiki/Patch_0.8.0.0 "Patch 0.8.0.0"): Introduced twelve new Crash Sites
  * [Patch 0.5.0.6](/wiki/Patch_0.5.0.6 "Patch 0.5.0.6"): Re-added 2 missing Crash Sites
  * [Patch 0.4.0.0](/wiki/Patch_0.4.0.0 "Patch 0.4.0.0"): Introduced seven new Crash Sites
  * [Patch 0.3](/wiki/Patch_0.3 "Patch 0.3"): Items are now inserted automatically when the hatch is opened rather than having to be dragged into a slot in the UI
  * Unknown Update 2 patch: Changed the unlock requirements of several Crash Sites, they no longer require items such as Uranium Fuel Rods
