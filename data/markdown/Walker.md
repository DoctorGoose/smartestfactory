## Walker

[ ](/wiki/File:Walker.png "Walker.png")

## Lifeform

### Hitpoints

100

### Damage

10

### Behavior

Hostile

The **Walker** was a one-eyed cyclops-like monster, which could be found in a cave in [Abyss Cliffs](/wiki/Abyss_Cliffs "Abyss Cliffs"). There was only one of this creature on the map, and it didn't respawn upon reloading the game. Additionally, it did not drop any items upon death. 

The enemy is from [Sanctum 2](https://sanctum.fandom.com/wiki/Sanctum_Wiki), another game by [Coffee Stain Studios](/wiki/Coffee_Stain_Studios "Coffee Stain Studios").

## Behavior

  * The Walker made a creepy screeching sound when attacking.[1]
  * If it was approached by the [pioneer](/wiki/Pioneer "Pioneer"), it would become aggressive. Its attack dealt 10 [damage](/wiki/Damage "Damage").
  * The monster triggered its attack by using both large hands.



## Trivia

  * On April 1, 2021, the April Fools Special Event was introduced. This event repeats on the 1st of April every year. During the event, the Walker Graphics Mesh is used to replace the [Fluffy-Tailed Hog](/wiki/Fluffy-tailed_Hog "Fluffy-tailed Hog") Graphic Mesh.
