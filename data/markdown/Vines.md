## Vines

[ ](/wiki/File:Vines.png "Vines.png")

_Primarily used for fuel. Biomass burners and vehicles can use it for power._

## Item

### [Stack size](/wiki/Stack "Stack")

200 

**Vines** used to be an organic item that could be harvested from certain plants. It could be used directly as fuel for [Biomass Burners](/wiki/Biomass_Burner "Biomass Burner"), but couldn't be turned into [](/wiki/Biomass "Biomass") [Biomass](/wiki/Biomass "Biomass"). 

## History

  * Unknown patch before [Patch 0.1](/wiki/Patch_0.1 "Patch 0.1"): Completely removed
  * [Patch Closed Alpha 4](/wiki/Patch_Closed_Alpha_4 "Patch Closed Alpha 4"): No longer attainable, although saves from before then may still have them in inventories
