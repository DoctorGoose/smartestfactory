The **Quantum Encoder** is a [building](/wiki/Building "Building") used for producing late-game items. Every recipe in the Quantum Encoder consumes [](/wiki/Excited_Photonic_Matter "Excited Photonic Matter") [Excited Photonic Matter](/wiki/Excited_Photonic_Matter "Excited Photonic Matter") and produces [](/wiki/Dark_Matter_Residue "Dark Matter Residue") [Dark Matter Residue](/wiki/Dark_Matter_Residue "Dark Matter Residue") in 1:1 ratios. 

It has a fluctuating power demand, considerably more than [Particle Accelerators](/wiki/Particle_Accelerator "Particle Accelerator").

## Overclocking

_Main article:_[Clock speed](/wiki/Clock_speed "Clock speed")

The Quantum Encoder can be overclocked using [Power Shards](/wiki/Power_Shard "Power Shard"). Overclocking increases the input/output speed of the Quantum Encoder at the cost of exponentially increased [power](/wiki/Power "Power") demand. 

## Power fluctuation

Unlike the [Particle Accelerator](/wiki/Particle_Accelerator "Particle Accelerator") and [Converter](/wiki/Converter "Converter") whose power consumption ramps up or down linearly, on the Quantum Encoder's power draw follows erratic pattern during the production cycle, instantly changing every 10% of each cycle. The average power consumption is 1000 MW, and the maximum is 2000 MW.

## History

  * [Patch 1.0](/wiki/Patch_1.0 "Patch 1.0")
    * Reintroduced with a different model which retains some of the inspiration about the old mesh like the arms.
    * The most high-tech production building in the game. The Quantum Encoder is used to produce the most complex parts, down to the quantum level, such as Superposition Oscillators and Neural-Quantum Processors.
    * The Quantum Encoder requires Excited Photonic Matter to operate and produces Dark Matter Residue as a byproduct.
  * [Patch 0.1.5](/wiki/Patch_0.1.5 "Patch 0.1.5"): Removed. Before its removal, its cost was 20 Heavy Modular Frames, 10 Crystal Oscillators, 10 Supercomputers and 10 High-Speed Connectors. It had 2 Conveyor inputs, 1 Conveyor output and a power usage of 40 MW.
  * Unknown Closed Alpha patch: Introduced, but unobtainable without save editing.
