The Elevatorusage template is used in the Usage section of parts under Space Elevator. Optional parameters are: `amt`, to display an amount to deliver, `name` (defaults to PAGENAME), for an alternative name to display, and `phase`, to show the phase used. 

## Output

**Elevatorusage** is required to complete deliveries in the [](/wiki/Space_Elevator "Space Elevator") [Space Elevator](/wiki/Space_Elevator "Space Elevator").
