This is a table detailing raw **resource renewability**. A raw resource is any resource that can be obtained from the world without being processed ([Crash Site](/wiki/Crash_Site "Crash Site") wreckage is not considered). 

Of all the items listed below, only [Hard Drives](/wiki/Hard_Drive "Hard Drive"), [Somersloops](/wiki/Somersloop "Somersloop"), and [Mercer Spheres](/wiki/Mercer_Sphere "Mercer Sphere") are non-renewable resources.
