# Patch 0.3.6.3

Patch Notes: Early Access (EXPERIMENTAL) – **v0.3.6.3** – Build 136090. This patch was released on 6 November 2020.   
  
The original post can be viewed on Satisfactory's [Discord](https://discord.com/channels/370472939054956546/557603412565426196/774337531625865277), [Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/SatisfactoryGame/comments/jpacgk), and [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/newshub/app/526870/view/2948130118090458987). 

## Intro

Hi Pioneers! 

I’m sure nobody was expecting the next patch to be that soon, were you? :D, just to give you all a little extra here’s some stuff to play around with before the weekend, as always your feedback means a lot to us so here’s some balancing for our latest additions and a few extra bug fixes 

If you happen to spot any issues please let us know at <https://questions.satisfactorygame.com/> we will appreciate reading all your posts with our morning coffee on Monday 

But until then, have a great weekend and keep enjoying the game, see you again soon! <3 

## Balancing

  * [Valve](/wiki/Valve "Valve") is now unlocked in [Tier 5](/wiki/Tier_5 "Tier 5"), Oil Processing. (Was previously in Tier 6, Expanded Power Infrastructure). The cost of the Valve is unchanged.
  * [Pipeline Mk.2](/wiki/Pipeline_Mk.2 "Pipeline Mk.2") and [Pump Mk.2](/wiki/Pipeline_Pump_Mk.2 "Pipeline Pump Mk.2") are now located in their own Milestone in [Tier 6](/wiki/Tier_6 "Tier 6"). (Were previously in [Tier 7](/wiki/Tier_7 "Tier 7"), Bauxite Refinement.) 
    * The new Milestone, 'Pipeline Engineering Mk.2', costs: 1000 [Copper Sheets](/wiki/Copper_Sheet "Copper Sheet"), 400 [Plastic](/wiki/Plastic "Plastic"), 400 [Rubber](/wiki/Rubber "Rubber"), 50 [Heavy Modular Frames](/wiki/Heavy_Modular_Frame "Heavy Modular Frame").
  * To fit the new unlock location Pipeline and Pump Mk.2 building costs have changed to: 
    * Pipeline Mk.2: 2 Copper Sheets + 1 Plastic
    * Pump Mk.2: 2 [Motor](/wiki/Motor "Motor") \+ 4 [Encased Industrial Beams](/wiki/Encased_Industrial_Beam "Encased Industrial Beam") \+ 8 Plastic
  * Pump Mk.2 [power](/wiki/Power "Power") consumption was reduced from 12 to 8 MW.



## Bug fixes

  * Fixed items being invisible near [crash sites](/wiki/Crash_site "Crash site") in certain parts of the map
  * Actually fixed the capitalization for [Coal](/wiki/Coal "Coal") in the [Resource Scanner](/wiki/Resource_Scanner "Resource Scanner")
