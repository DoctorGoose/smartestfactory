## Location

A Manta can be found circling around the following biomes: 

  * [Grass Fields](/wiki/Grass_Fields "Grass Fields")
  * [Northern Forest](/wiki/Northern_Forest "Northern Forest")



[](/wiki/File:Manta_path.png)

[](/wiki/File:Manta_path.png "Enlarge")

The paths of the two Mantas on the [map](/wiki/Map "Map"). Both circuits take exactly five minutes.

## Behavior

Giant Flying Manta glide through the sky and are accompanied by swarms of [small flying creatures (birds)](/wiki/Flying_Bird "Flying Bird"). Each manta patrols on a fixed path. The round-trip time for each manta is exactly five minutes. The manta and its flock are immune to the pioneer's attacks. [Pioneers](/wiki/Pioneer "Pioneer") can jump onto its back and take a ride. Doing so generally requires a [](/wiki/Jetpack "Jetpack") [Jetpack](/wiki/Jetpack "Jetpack") or tall [](/wiki/Ladder "Ladder") [Ladder](/wiki/Ladder "Ladder"). Take caution as the pioneer can only stay on the center part of its body, standing too near to the edge of the wing can cause pioneers to fall and suffer massive [fall damage](/wiki/Fall_damage "Fall damage"). 

When flying, mantas avoid all the terrain features, such as flying above small hills, dive into ravines, passing through stone arches, and spiralling around rock spires. By riding on the manta, pioneers have the opportunity to discover interesting terrain features, such as [Resource Nodes](/wiki/Resource_Node "Resource Node"), [](/wiki/Power_Slug "Power Slug") [Power Slugs](/wiki/Power_Slug "Power Slug"), [](/wiki/Crash_Site "Crash Site") [Crash Sites](/wiki/Crash_Site "Crash Site"), [](/wiki/Mercer_Sphere "Mercer Sphere") [Mercer Spheres](/wiki/Mercer_Sphere "Mercer Sphere") and [](/wiki/Somersloop "Somersloop") [Somersloops](/wiki/Somersloop "Somersloop"). 

If man-made buildings are built in its path, it will simply fly through them as there is no collision between the Manta and the buildings. The pioneer, however, if standing on it, does collide with the buildings and will be pushed away from Manta. 

Its body emits blue lights during the night. 

### Riding

Be sure to stand near the center of its back to avoid falls. The Manta around the Northern Forest tilts more, so its safe spot is narrower. Reloading the game while riding will reset the Manta's position to its spawn point, causing the pioneer to fall. 

The Manta can be pet by pressing [`E`](/wiki/Controls "Controls").

## Trivia

  * Unlike a pioneer, vehicles cannot be on the surface of Manta’s skin, as they fall through it.
  * Its name comes from the Patch 0.1 patch notes
  * It resembles a much larger version of the Skyray from the survival game Subnautica.
  * Jace & Snutt claim that the Manta feels pain when clipping through player's factories.[1]
  * Jace joked that the Manta's name is Marty.[2]
  * Pioneers can pet the Manta by pressing [`E`](/wiki/Controls "Controls") while looking at it.[3]
