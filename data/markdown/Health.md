The health bar

**Health** points (**HP** or **hitpoints**) refers to the amount of damage one can take before dying. 

Below is the list of things with health points: 

  * [Pioneers](/wiki/Pioneer "Pioneer")
  * [All hostile creatures](/wiki/Combat#Hostile_creatures "Combat")
  * [](/wiki/Lizard_Doggo "Lizard Doggo") [Lizzard Doggos](/wiki/Lizard_Doggo "Lizard Doggo"), [](/wiki/Beetle "Beetle") [Beetles](/wiki/Beetle "Beetle"), [](/wiki/Space_Giraffe-Tick-Penguin-Whale_Thing "Space Giraffe-Tick-Penguin-Whale Thing") [Space Giraffe-Tick-Penguin-Whale Things](/wiki/Space_Giraffe-Tick-Penguin-Whale_Thing "Space Giraffe-Tick-Penguin-Whale Thing") and [](/wiki/Non_Flying_Bird "Non Flying Bird") [Non Flying Birds](/wiki/Non_Flying_Bird "Non Flying Bird")
  * [](/wiki/Spore_Flower "Spore Flower") [Spore Flowers](/wiki/Spore_Flower "Spore Flower") and [Gas Pillars](/wiki/Gas_Pillar "Gas Pillar")



Other objects, such as [player built structures](/wiki/Buildings "Buildings"), do not have hit points and cannot be damaged by any means. 

The player-controlled character, [pioneer](/wiki/Pioneer "Pioneer"), has `100` health points divided into `10` segments in the [HUD](/wiki/HUD "HUD"), of `10` HP each.

## Taking damage

Every time the pioneer takes damage from any source, the sound of a heart monitor beeping will play and a flashing white message will display on the top of the screen reading: 

“  | _DAMAGE TO FICSIT PROPERTY DETECTED_ | „   
---|---|---  
  
If the pioneer falls below `30` health, the message will turn orange and read: 

“  | _IMMINENT DESTRUCTION OF FICSIT PROPERTY DETECTED_ | „   
---|---|---  
  
The message will go away after not taking damage for a couple of seconds.  


The heart monitor is the only sound that plays, there are no injury sounds or gasps like you might see in other games. 

## Death

If the health reaches `0`, the pioneer dies. The sound of a heartrate monitor flatlining plays, the camera switches to 3rd person and the character enters a rag-doll state. A prompt will pop up which allows the pioneer to be re-spawned, by pressing [](/wiki/File:Keyboard_White_Mouse_Right.png "Right")[`Right click`](/wiki/Controls "Controls"). The player will then be re-spawned at [The HUB](/wiki/The_HUB "The HUB") with `30` health points.  
  
Based on [options](/wiki/Options "Options"), either all items excluding equipped [equipment](/wiki/Equipment "Equipment"), all items, or nothing will drop into a death [](/wiki/Crate "Crate") [crate](/wiki/Crate "Crate") at the location of the pioneer's corpse. If falling into the [void](/wiki/Void "Void"), the [](/wiki/Crate "Crate") [crate](/wiki/Crate "Crate") will appear where the pioneer last touched the ground. The [](/wiki/Crate "Crate") [crate](/wiki/Crate "Crate") stays there forever until emptied by the player; however, its marker disappears from the compass once the game is reloaded.  
  
In a [multiplayer](/wiki/Multiplayer "Multiplayer") session, other pioneers can revive dead pioneers by holding down [`E`](/wiki/Controls "Controls") over them for five seconds, respawning them with `30` HP.  
  
Death is referred to as `expiration` by [ADA](/wiki/ADA "ADA"). 

## Regeneration

When health is below `30`, the health bar will start flashing red. If the pioneer does not receive damage for `5` seconds, emergency health regen of `1.25HP/sec` will take place up to `30` HP, followed by slow regen up to full health.  
  
Healing items may be consumed to restore health, up to `100` (full health). Healing items can be consumed even at full health, regenerating nothing. To consume a healing item, place it in your hands and click the [](/wiki/File:Keyboard_White_Mouse_Left.png "Left")[`Left click`](/wiki/Controls "Controls").

### Fall damage

Falling from a height of more than `13.5` meters results in fall damage. Fall damage can be prevented with an [U-Jelly Landing Pad](/wiki/U-Jelly_Landing_Pad "U-Jelly Landing Pad"), [Parachute](/wiki/Parachute "Parachute") or [Jetpack](/wiki/Jetpack "Jetpack"). Fall damage is slightly reduced when a pair of [Blade Runners](/wiki/Blade_Runners "Blade Runners") is equipped. Fall damage is capped at `97` damage, with Blade Runners at `96`, therefore all falls are not lethal provided the pioneer has `98–100` HP before touching the ground. 

  * [](/wiki/File:Fall_damage_data.png "Fall damage comparison between wearing Blade Runners vs not. Around the 50 meters mark, wearing Blade Runners actually causes higher fall damage.")

Fall damage comparison between wearing Blade Runners vs not. Around the 50 meters mark, wearing Blade Runners actually causes higher fall damage.




However, note that fall damage is calculated based on impact velocity and angle, not merely distance. Notably, a [Hypertube](/wiki/Hypertube "Hypertube") exit pointed towards the ground can cause significant fall damage. Thus, Hypertube exits should always face into a wall or an U-Jelly Landing Pad.

## History

  * [Patch 0.6.0.4](/wiki/Patch_0.6.0.4 "Patch 0.6.0.4"): Reduced Pioneer passive health regen rate in the 30% to 100% range from 0.25 to 0.1 recovery per second, effectively increasing health regen duration until full from ~5 minutes to ~12 minutes
  * [Patch 0.6.0.0](/wiki/Patch_0.6.0.0 "Patch 0.6.0.0"): Pioneers now passively regenerate to full health.
  * [Patch 0.3](/wiki/Patch_0.3 "Patch 0.3"): Reviving an pioneer in multiplayer now spawns them with 30 HP instead of 1 HP.
  * [Patch Closed Alpha 5](/wiki/Patch_Closed_Alpha_5 "Patch Closed Alpha 5"): Pioneers no longer respawn with full health.
