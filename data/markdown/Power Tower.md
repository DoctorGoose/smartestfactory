The **Power Tower** and **Power Tower Platform** are [power grid](/wiki/Power "Power") structures suited for running [Power Lines](/wiki/Power_Lines "Power Lines") over longer distances.

## Construction

Power Towers have two power connectors.[1] The top connector is visually represented by two Power Lines, but behaves and costs as much as one.[2] It allows three Power Line connections to other Power Towers over an extended distance of 300 meters, which costs 12 [](/wiki/Cable "Cable") [Cables](/wiki/Cable "Cable"). Clicking on the top connector drags out another Power Tower. 

The bottom connector allows four connections to other [Power Poles](/wiki/Power_Poles "Power Poles") or other buildings. Clicking on the bottom connector drags out a standard [Mk.1 Power Pole](/wiki/Power_Poles#Mk.1-0 "Power Poles"). 

While drawing out a Power Line from the top connector, the building range is increased to 300 m. However, the range is not extended when aiming at a distant Power Tower. Thus, when connecting Power Towers, first select the closer tower and then the remote one. 

Like [Foundations](/wiki/Foundations "Foundations"), Power Towers can use gradual rotation when placing by using [`Ctrl`](/wiki/Controls "Controls"). 

### Upgrading

Power Towers can be upgraded in-place to the Power Tower Platform and vice versa, similar to [Conveyor Belts](/wiki/Conveyor_Belts "Conveyor Belts") and [Power Poles](/wiki/Power_Poles "Power Poles"), meaning that they do not have to be dismantled to switch between variants. 

## Usage

As mentioned, the extended range is useful for easily constructing long [Power Lines](/wiki/Power_Line "Power Line"). While [Railways](/wiki/Railway "Railway") also transmit power, they are unlocked two tiers later, and your destination might not be worth connecting by rail (such as a remote power plant or [Miner](/wiki/Miner "Miner")). 

Both types of Power Towers are functionally identical, but the Platform variant has an additional ladder and platform near the upper connectors, which makes them ideal for [ziplining](/wiki/Zipline "Zipline"). 

[Walkways](/wiki/Walkways "Walkways") and [Catwalks](/wiki/Walkways "Walkways") can be snapped to Platform Power Towers, akin to [Lookout Towers](/wiki/Lookout_Tower "Lookout Tower").[3]
