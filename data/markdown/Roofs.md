**Roofs** are a set of [buildings](/wiki/Buildings "Buildings") which can be used to cover open areas of a factory. They can be [painted](/wiki/Painted "Painted"), like other buildings. Roofs snap to other [Walls](/wiki/Walls "Walls"). They are useful for decorating structures.

## Types

All roofs share the following common properties: 

  * Width: 8 m
  * Length: 8 m
  * Height: 0.5-4 m (depends on angle)
  * Build cost: 
    * 2× [Customizer](/wiki/Customizer "Customizer") Material
    * 3× [](/wiki/Concrete "Concrete") [Concrete](/wiki/Concrete "Concrete")

## Construction

  * Roofs can be built one at a time or 10 at once using the [Zoop](/wiki/Build_Gun#Zoop "Build Gun") construction mode.



## Tips

  * Flat Roofs, which have a height of 0.51m, get placed in the center of a snapping point, meaning it will be 25.5cm above a [Foundation](/wiki/Foundation "Foundation"). By using a combination of [Pillars](/wiki/Pillars "Pillars"), [Foundations](/wiki/Foundations "Foundations") and [Frame Floors](/wiki/Frame_Structures "Frame Structures") you can create foundations at any 1cm interval



## History

  * [Patch 0.7.0.6](/wiki/Patch_0.7.0.6 "Patch 0.7.0.6"): Fixed [Conveyor Floor Holes](/wiki/Conveyor_Lift_Floor_Hole "Conveyor Lift Floor Hole") Conveyor Floor Holes and [Pipe Floor Holes](/wiki/Pipeline_Floor_Hole "Pipeline Floor Hole") not working when snapped to Roof foundations
  * [Patch 0.5.0.0](/wiki/Patch_0.5.0.0 "Patch 0.5.0.0"): Introduced 
    * Flat, 1- , 2- , and 4- meter versions
    * Introduced [Customizer](/wiki/Customizer "Customizer") FICSIT, Tar, Steel, and Glass materials for roofs
    * Roofs cost 3 [Concrete](/wiki/Concrete "Concrete") \+ Material Cost
