# Copper Sheet

## Copper Sheet

[ ](/wiki/File:Copper_Sheet.png "Copper Sheet.png")

_Used for crafting.  
Primarily used for Pipelines due to its high corrosion resistance._

### Unlocked by

[Tier 2](/wiki/Tier_2 "Tier 2") \- Part Assembly

### Class name

Desc_CopperSheet_C

## Item

### Stack size

200

### [Sink points](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink")

24

**Copper Sheet** is an early-game item used primarily in the construction of [Pipelines](/wiki/Pipelines "Pipelines") and fluid-related [buildings](/wiki/Buildings "Buildings"). It is the most basic product from [Copper](/wiki/Copper_Ore "Copper Ore"), alongside [Wires](/wiki/Wire "Wire").

### AWESOME Sink

**[](/wiki/Copper_Sheet "Copper Sheet")Copper Sheet** can be sunk into the [](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") [AWESOME Sink](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") to yield **24 points**.

## Tips

  * Copper Sheets can be produced very efficiently using the [Pure Copper Ingot](/wiki/Copper_Ingot "Copper Ingot") and Steamed Copper Sheet alt recipes; 2.5 sheets per ore compared to 0.5 for the default recipes, just by adding water.
