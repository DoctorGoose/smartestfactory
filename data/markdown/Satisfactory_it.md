## Satisfactory

_**Satisfactory**_ is a first-person factory construction and automation game with single player and co-op [multiplayer](/wiki/Multiplayer "Multiplayer"), made by [Coffee Stain Studios](/wiki/Coffee_Stain_Studios "Coffee Stain Studios") in [Unreal Engine 5](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Unreal_Engine#Unreal_Engine_5 "wikipedia:Unreal Engine").[4] You play as an employee of [FICSIT Inc.](/wiki/FICSIT_Inc. "FICSIT Inc.") and must contribute towards [Project Assembly](/wiki/Space_Elevator "Space Elevator"). 

As a [pioneer](/wiki/Pioneer "Pioneer"), you are tasked with exploring the [planet](/wiki/World "World") for resources, in order to construct [machines](/wiki/Buildings "Buildings"), which will build necessary parts. As the game progresses, more complex machines and parts become available. 

The game was initially released in Early Access exclusively on the [Epic Games Store](https://www.epicgames.com/store/en-US/product/satisfactory/) on March 19, 2019 at 5pm GMT. It later released on [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/526870/Satisfactory/) on June 8, 2020 at 5pm GMT, with cross-platform multiplayer. Version 1.0 was launched on September 10, 2024 at 5pm GMT on the Epic Games Store and Steam, with a console release announced for a later but as of now undetermined date. 

More information can be found at the [official site](https://www.satisfactorygame.com/) or on the [Dev Tracker](https://devtrackers.gg/satisfactory). 

## Official description

_Satisfactory_ is a first-person open-world factory building game with a dash of exploration and combat. Pioneering for [FICSIT Incorporated](/wiki/FICSIT_Inc. "FICSIT Inc.") means charting and exploiting an alien planet, battling alien lifeforms, creating multi-story factories, entering [conveyor belt](/wiki/Conveyor_Belt "Conveyor Belt") heaven, automating [vehicles](/wiki/Vehicles "Vehicles"), and [researching](/wiki/M.A.M "M.A.M") new [technologies](/wiki/Milestones "Milestones"). 

At FICSIT inc. you will take part in the Save the Day program, providing short-term solutions for long-term problems, possibly with the help of your friends.

## Monetization

The game is a one-time purchase, and will not feature micro-transactions or loot boxes. There are plans to support the game post release, possibly with updates and/or DLC. It is unknown if these will be free or paid.[5]

On 5 August 2024, before the release of [1.0](/wiki/1.0 "1.0"), the price was increased from $29.99 to $39.99.[3] The reason given is inflation and development time spent since the original release.[6]

## Trivia

  * While in pre-Alpha, between 16-18 people worked on Satisfactory's development.[7]
  * Two out of three releases were made on time in 2020.[8]
  * Staff at Coffee Stain were surprised to learn that around the time of the Early Access launch, Satisfactory had more concurrent views than DOTA 2 (a major e-sports title).[9]
  * Satisfactory's name was hinted at in a 2017 tweet,[10] and later in a 2018 Q&A video.[11]
  * Satisfactory's pre-production started around 2016/2017.[12]
    * The game's development began with Unreal Engine 4 and continued to use it until Update 8, released in 2023, when the game upgraded to Unreal Engine 5.[4]
  * By December 2021, Satisfactory had sold 3 million copies.[13] As of December 2023, Satisfactory had sold 5.5 million copies.[14]
