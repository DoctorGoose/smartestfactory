# Wire

## Wire

[ ](/wiki/File:Wire.png "Wire.png")

_Used for crafting.  
One of the most basic parts._

### Unlocked by

[Tier 0](/wiki/Tier_0 "Tier 0") \- HUB Upgrade 2

### Class name

## Item

### Stack size

500

### [Sink points](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink")

6

**Wire** is the most basic product from [Copper](/wiki/Copper_Ore "Copper Ore"), alongside [Copper Sheets](/wiki/Copper_Sheet "Copper Sheet"). It is commonly used in [power](/wiki/Power "Power") infrastructure.
