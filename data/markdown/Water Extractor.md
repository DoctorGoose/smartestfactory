The **Water Extractor** is a [Building](/wiki/Building "Building") used to pump [Water](/wiki/Water "Water") out of water bodies. It has one [Pipeline](/wiki/Pipeline "Pipeline") output with a recommended head lift of 10 meters. 

It cannot extract Water from [Resource Wells](/wiki/Resource_Well "Resource Well"); a [Resource Well Pressurizer](/wiki/Resource_Well_Pressurizer "Resource Well Pressurizer") and [Extractor](/wiki/Resource_Well_Extractor "Resource Well Extractor") have to be used instead.

## Construction

  * The building's hitbox is about 20 m x 19.5 m, but the space required to place it down is 25 m x 25 m. Thus, it is recommended to build the Water Extractor first, then build any desired [Foundations](/wiki/Foundations "Foundations") around it.
  * The Water Extractor can be snapped to [Foundations](/wiki/Foundations "Foundations") or other extractors when it is being placed.
  * Sometimes, it can be difficult to place the Water Extractor down due to the depth of the water and the view angle. It is advised to stand on higher elevation such as on a [Lookout Tower](/wiki/Lookout_Tower "Lookout Tower") or [ladders](/wiki/Ladder "Ladder") before building the Extractors.



## Water depth

  * Water Extractors must be built on deep water. When building it, a warning will display if the target water is too shallow. Rivers are usually considered shallow water.
  * Water Extractors need a vertical clearance of at least nine meters below the water surface. This refers to man-made buildings only. For example, if a Foundation is located nine meters below the water level, an extractor can then be built above it.
  * There are exceptions: as the deep water area classified by the internal game code does not totally match the terrain appearance, there are some areas where Water Extractor cannot be built on them, in which case one must find another water source. However, there are some areas where you can build water extractors despite possessing an inadequate depth of water, or no water at all.



### Disallowed areas

After a certain distance, water in these areas no longer counts as a water volume for purposes of building Extractors. A build hologram will not appear when attempting to place a Water Extractor beyond this boundary. 

  * West of the Oil [Islands](/wiki/Islands "Islands") coast.
  * South-East of the [Swamp](/wiki/Swamp "Swamp").



## Snapping

To snap Water Extractors to a foundation grid, you must be pointing at a foundation while placing the Water Extractor. The following technique can be used to make this easier: 

  1. Build a [Foundation 8m x 4m](/wiki/Foundation_8m_x_4m "Foundation 8m x 4m") where you want the output Pipeline to connect. The top of this foundation should be close to the water surface.
  2. Build a second [Foundation 8m x 4m](/wiki/Foundation_8m_x_4m "Foundation 8m x 4m") on the far side of where the Water Extractor will be placed. Make sure to leave three foundations of space between the foundations.
  3. Stand on the first foundation.
  4. Build a Water Extractor while aiming at the second foundation.



The Water Extractor should snap to the same grid as the second foundation. If you want to snap to the grid of the first foundation make sure it is on the same grid as the second foundation. 

This also works from the side, but can be trickier to line up as the snapping point is off-center. 

Additionally, holding [`Ctrl`](/wiki/Controls "Controls") allows the Water Extractors to be snapped next to each other. 

Holding [`Ctrl`](/wiki/Controls "Controls") also allows Water Extractors to be aligned to a nearby [Pipeline Junction Cross](/wiki/Pipeline_Junction_Cross "Pipeline Junction Cross"). 

## Head lift

_Main article:_[Head lift](/wiki/Head_lift "Head lift")

A Water Extractor provides a [head lift](/wiki/Head_lift "Head lift") of 10 meters, measured from the center point of its pipe outlet. 

## Overclocking

_Main article:_[Clock Speed](/wiki/Clock_Speed "Clock Speed")

Water Extractors can be overclocked by using [Power Shards](/wiki/Power_Shard "Power Shard"). At 250% clock speed, a Water Extractor produces exactly as much flow as a [Pipeline Mk.1](/wiki/Pipeline_Mk.1 "Pipeline Mk.1") can transport.
