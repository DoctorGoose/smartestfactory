# Mercer Sphere

## Mercer Sphere

[ ](/wiki/File:Mercer_Sphere.png "Mercer Sphere.png")

_A weird alien phenomenon. While the sphere appears liquid, it behaves like a solid and does not have any detectable mass. It seems to somehow interact with and have awareness of its environment._

### Class name

Desc_WAT2_C

## Item

### Stack size

50

**Mercer Spheres** are alien artifacts found throughout the [world](/wiki/World "World") used to construct and research improvements for [](/wiki/Dimensional_Depot_Uploader "Dimensional Depot Uploader") [Dimensional Depot Uploaders](/wiki/Dimensional_Depot_Uploader "Dimensional Depot Uploader"). 

They are found on their own Mercer Sphere plant, from which they can be collected. Standing next to one for an extended period of time will result in a sound file being played. 

In closed alpha versions of the game, certain in-game messages would be received from d.m.mercer@ficsit.biz. In-universe, these spheres are named after this character (Dr. D. M. Mercer).

## Obtaining

Mercer Spheres can be found while exploring, typically in hard-to-reach places usually guarded by enemies and surrounded by [Uranium Deposits](/wiki/Resource_Node#Resource_Deposit "Resource Node"), [Spore Flowers](/wiki/Spore_Flower "Spore Flower"), [Cracked boulders](/wiki/Cracked_boulder "Cracked boulder") or [Gas Pillars](/wiki/Gas_Pillar "Gas Pillar"). 

There are a total of 298 Mercer Spheres as of [Patch 1.0](/wiki/Patch_1.0 "Patch 1.0"). 

A total of 97 Mercer Spheres are required to unlock all related research nodes in the [MAM](/wiki/MAM "MAM"). This leaves 201 Mercer Spheres available for other uses, such as constructing [](/wiki/Dimensional_Depot_Uploader "Dimensional Depot Uploader") [Dimensional Depot Uploaders](/wiki/Dimensional_Depot_Uploader "Dimensional Depot Uploader").

## Trivia

  * The Mercer Spheres were named after [Matthew Mercer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matthew_Mercer "wikipedia:Matthew Mercer"), an American voice actor and host of the Dungeons and Dragons web series Critical Role.[1]
    * The [Achievement](/wiki/Achievements "Achievements") for picking up a Mercer Sphere references this, named "I'm sure these play a Critical Role", with the icon being a 20-sided dice.
    * This is also referenced by ADA when first picking one up, attributing their discovery to a "D.M." (for Dungeon Master) Mercer.
    * Mercer himself discovered that a game element had been named after him when playing Satisfactory on stream with his wife.
  * The Mercer Sphere’s speech is extremely similar to the [Mesmers](https://subnautica.fandom.com/wiki/Mesmer) in Subnautica.



**Expand to reveal spoiler**

Mercer Spheres appear to be some sort of seed, and most likely the source of the large stony tendrils that cover the map. This theory is supported by several locations where the Spheres have shed their outer covering and have started to grow plant-like vines which resemble the larger terrain structures.
