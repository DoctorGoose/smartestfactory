# Crate

## Crate

[ ](/wiki/File:Crate.png "Crate.png")

_Stores items temporarily._

A **crate** is a type of temporary storage. It appears when the [pioneer](/wiki/Pioneer "Pioneer") respawns or if there is insufficient amount of empty slots in the [inventory](/wiki/Inventory "Inventory") when [dismantling](/wiki/Build_Gun "Build Gun").

## Death crate

A crate that appears after the pioneer dies and respawns if their "Keep Inventory" gameplay setting is either "Keep Nothing" or "Keep Equipment". It will contain all items from the pioneer's inventory as well items from equipment slots (unless "Keep Equipment" option is selected). If the pioneer dies without carrying any items or the "Keep Everything" option is selected, no crate will be created. Death crate locations and directions are indicated on the [Map](/wiki/Map "Map") and [Compass](/wiki/Compass "Compass") in the "Death Crates" category and can be toggled on or off. The crate usually appears at the location of player's death, however if the pioneer dies in the void or due to border [damage](/wiki/Damage "Damage"), the crate will be spawned on the latest spot where the pioneer stood on the ground. For example, if the pioneer dies at the map border due to being launched too far with a [Hyper Tube Cannon](/wiki/Hyper_Tube_Cannon "Hyper Tube Cannon"), the death crate will spawn at the launching point. 

In a multiplayer game, reviving an pioneer won't spawn a crate, as all inventory contents and equipment remain in the inventory of the revived character. 

## Dismantle crate

_Main article:_[ Settings > Gameplay > Gameplay](/wiki/Settings#Gameplay_2 "Settings")

A dismantle crate will appear if there are insufficient inventory slots to receive the building refund material. This includes the solid items stored in the building input and output slots, and the cost of [Power Lines](/wiki/Power_Line "Power Line") connected to it. 

The location of these crates is based upon the settings for both **Dismantle Crate Placement** and **Merge Dismantle Crates** _(see main article above)._

These crates also appear in the "Dismantle Crates" category on the Map and Compass and can be toggled on or off. 

## Common properties

Both types of the crates above share the following properties: 

  * The inventory size of the crate is automatically sized to contain the necessary items.
  * You can transfer items from the crate to the pioneer's inventory but not the other way round.
  * Once all its contained items are picked up, the crate is automatically removed. A crate cannot be dismantled by the pioneer.
  * Crates cannot be built manually.
  * A crate does not have an encroachment box. This means any buildings can be built intersect with it without issue.
  * Crates, along with contained items, never despawn.



## Trivia

  * The maximum possible amount of stacks a crate can store is 2451, which can be achieved by mass dismantling 50 full [Freight Platforms](/wiki/Freight_Platform "Freight Platform"): 
    * 2400 stacks of any item
    * 6 stacks of [](/wiki/Heavy_Modular_Frame "Heavy Modular Frame") [Heavy Modular Frames](/wiki/Heavy_Modular_Frame "Heavy Modular Frame")
    * 2 stacks of [](/wiki/Computer "Computer") [Computers](/wiki/Computer "Computer")
    * 25 stacks of [](/wiki/Concrete "Concrete") [Concrete](/wiki/Concrete "Concrete")
    * 12.5 (rounded up to 13) stacks of [](/wiki/Cable "Cable") [Cables](/wiki/Cable "Cable")
    * 5 stacks of [](/wiki/Motor "Motor") [Motors](/wiki/Motor "Motor")



## History

  * [Patch 1.0](/wiki/Patch_1.0 "Patch 1.0") Changed compass name depending on how it was spawned, such as "Death Crate" and "Dismantle Crate".
  * [Patch 0.6.0.3](/wiki/Patch_0.6.0.3 "Patch 0.6.0.3"): Fixed empty player crates lingering in the world and creating other issues when interacting with them
  * [Patch 0.6.0.2](/wiki/Patch_0.6.0.2 "Patch 0.6.0.2")
    * Fixed interacting with a Player Crate locking player input
    * Fixed Player Crates not disappearing after being emptied
  * [Patch 0.3](/wiki/Patch_0.3 "Patch 0.3"): Mass dismantling buildings now creates a single crate when the resources don’t fit into the inventory, instead of a crate per buildable dismantled
  * [Patch 0.1.5](/wiki/Patch_0.1.5 "Patch 0.1.5"): Hub parts are now returned to the player on respawn or passed to other players if possible when their death crate is spawned
  * [Patch Closed Alpha 5.1](/wiki/Patch_Closed_Alpha_5.1 "Patch Closed Alpha 5.1"): Changed Death Crates to only disappear from the Compass when emptied
  * [Patch Closed Alpha 3.2](/wiki/Patch_Closed_Alpha_3.2 "Patch Closed Alpha 3.2"): Fixed a crash when a too large death create is created
  * [Patch Closed Alpha 2](/wiki/Patch_Closed_Alpha_2 "Patch Closed Alpha 2"): Dismantle/death crate: Inventory will not be squished anymore when extremely full.
