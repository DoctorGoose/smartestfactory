The **Personal Storage Box** is a small container for storing items. It is exactly 100 times smaller than a [Storage Container](/wiki/Storage_Container "Storage Container") at 2 m3, has one more storage slot (25), and lacks [conveyor](/wiki/Conveyor "Conveyor") ports, making it only accessible to [pioneers](/wiki/Pioneer "Pioneer").

## HUB Storage Box

A Personal Storage Box can be found in [the HUB](/wiki/The_HUB "The HUB") once unlocked in [Tier 0](/wiki/Tier_0 "Tier 0"). It cannot be moved, dismantled, or copied with the building eyedropper. 

## Trivia

  * Despite its name "Personal" Storage Box, it can be accessed by all players in a multiplayer session.
  * Personal Storage Boxes can be stacked vertically, 1 m apart from each other. This can be achieved by constructing stacked 8x1m [Foundations](/wiki/Foundations "Foundations"), then building the upper Storage Box and dismantling the upper Foundation. The lowest Storage Box goes last.
  * Additionally, they can also be placed on the sides of a Foundation (but not a wall), snapping horizontally but not vertically, 1 m apart.
