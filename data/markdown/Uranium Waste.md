# Uranium Waste

## Uranium Waste

[ ](/wiki/File:Uranium_Waste.png "Uranium Waste.png")

_The by-product of consuming Uranium Fuel Rods in a Nuclear Power Plant.  
Non-Fissile Uranium can be extracted. Handle with caution.  
Caution: HIGHLY Radioactive._

### Class name

Desc_NuclearWaste_C

## Item

### Stack size

500

### [Radioactive](/wiki/Radioactivity "Radioactivity")

10

**Uranium Waste** is a [radioactive](/wiki/Radioactive "Radioactive") byproduct of [](/wiki/Nuclear_Power_Plant "Nuclear Power Plant") [Nuclear Power Plants](/wiki/Nuclear_Power_Plant "Nuclear Power Plant") by burning [](/wiki/Uranium_Fuel_Rod "Uranium Fuel Rod") [Uranium Fuel Rods](/wiki/Uranium_Fuel_Rod "Uranium Fuel Rod"). 

It can be reprocessed into [](/wiki/Plutonium_Fuel_Rod "Plutonium Fuel Rod") [Plutonium Fuel Rods](/wiki/Plutonium_Fuel_Rod "Plutonium Fuel Rod"), which can be [sunk](/wiki/Sink "Sink"), or burned for power where it creates [](/wiki/Plutonium_Waste "Plutonium Waste") [Plutonium Waste](/wiki/Plutonium_Waste "Plutonium Waste"). 

It is one of the few items that **cannot be discarded with the trash button** in containers or the inventory, giving a message that "Nuclear Waste cannot be destroyed. FICSIT does not waste."

### AWESOME Sink

**[](/wiki/Uranium_Waste "Uranium Waste")Uranium Waste** cannot be sunk into the [](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") [AWESOME Sink](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") and will clog the input.

## Dealing with Uranium Waste

There are two ways of legitimately dealing with Uranium Waste: 

  * Storing Uranium Waste in a giant complex of [Storage Containers](/wiki/Storage_Container "Storage Container")
    * This is not as effective as storing [](/wiki/Plutonium_Waste "Plutonium Waste") [Plutonium Waste](/wiki/Plutonium_Waste "Plutonium Waste") as the conversion ratios of Uranium Waste to Plutonium Waste will always leave you with less Plutonium Waste produced per minute.
  * Making [](/wiki/Plutonium_Fuel_Rod "Plutonium Fuel Rod") [Plutonium Fuel Rods](/wiki/Plutonium_Fuel_Rod "Plutonium Fuel Rod")
    * Sinking the Plutonium Fuel Rods is one of the [two](/wiki/Plutonium_Waste#Dealing_with_Plutonium_Waste "Plutonium Waste") ways to eliminate nuclear waste, this method also generates good amount of points in the [AWESOME Sink](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink").
    * Using Plutonium Fuel Rods as fuel for [Vehicles](/wiki/Vehicles "Vehicles") OR [Drones](/wiki/Drone "Drone").
    * Burning the Plutonium Fuel Rods in a [](/wiki/Nuclear_Power_Plant "Nuclear Power Plant") [Nuclear Power Plant](/wiki/Nuclear_Power_Plant "Nuclear Power Plant") produces a good amount of power, but it generates [](/wiki/Plutonium_Waste "Plutonium Waste") [Plutonium Waste](/wiki/Plutonium_Waste "Plutonium Waste").



See also: [Dealing with Plutonium Waste](/wiki/Plutonium_Waste#Dealing_with_Plutonium_Waste "Plutonium Waste")
