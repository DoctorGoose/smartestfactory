# Patch 0.6.0.12

Patch Notes: Early Access (EXPERIMENTAL) – **v0.6.0.12** – Build 199356. This patch was released on 6 September 2022.   
  
The original post can be viewed on Satisfactory's [Discord](https://discord.com/channels/370472939054956546/557603412565426196/1016807305028841503), [Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/SatisfactoryGame/comments/x7kx7l), and [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/news/app/526870/view/3354640305209873543).

## Intro

Hi Pioneers! 

Hello again everybody, Turns out there were some more crashes as a side effect of the last few patches so here’s some fixes along with a bunch of goodies :) 

If we introduced yet another crash or any new issues please let us know over at our QA Site: <https://questions.satisfactorygame.com/> We’ll look into it ASAP 

See you all again soon <3 

## New

  * Pretty stuff in the [skybox](/wiki/World#Day_and_night_cycle "World")



## Creatures

  * Adjustments to the Spitter VFX, Animations, AI and Attacks
  * Adjustments to [Fluffy-tailed Hogs](/wiki/Fluffy-tailed_Hog "Fluffy-tailed Hog") and [Alpha Hogs](/wiki/Alpha_Hog "Alpha Hog") Physics, AI and Attacks



## Bug fixes

  * Fixed crash related to Hatchers on [Dedicated Server](/wiki/Dedicated_Server "Dedicated Server")
  * Fixed up Low spec Skybox
  * Potential fix to a crash related to DroneAction
  * Potential fix for creatures becoming stiff when ragdolling
  * Fixed player placement while riding the [Explorer](/wiki/Explorer "Explorer")
  * Fixed an issue where the player could open the [Customizer](/wiki/Customizer "Customizer") menu before it was unlocked
  * Fixed [Crab Hatcher](/wiki/Flying_Crab_Hatcher "Flying Crab Hatcher") not spawning crabs in certain scenarios
  * Fixed [headlift](/wiki/Head_lift "Head lift") issues when using [pipe floor holes](/wiki/Pipeline_Floor_Hole "Pipeline Floor Hole")
  * Modified pipe hologram so that it now looks better when running from a [Floor Hole](/wiki/Pipeline_Supports "Pipeline Supports") to a [pipe pole](/wiki/Pipeline_Supports "Pipeline Supports")



## UI

  * Increased [build menu](/wiki/Build_Gun "Build Gun") size to fit 6 items in a single row (Was 5 before)
  * Shortened build menu Category "Transportation" to "Transport"
  * Updated visuals for [Multiplayer](/wiki/Multiplayer "Multiplayer") and [Dedicated servers](/wiki/Dedicated_servers "Dedicated servers") offline player name tags
  * Fixed issue where consumables wouldn’t show their total amount when holstered
  * Fixed a bug where Far/Infinite settings on the [Map](/wiki/Map "Map") markers were switched
  * Fixed an issue where the weapon crosshair would display while the Build Gun is active
  * Updated icons in the [Signs Icon Library](/wiki/Signs "Signs") to add new content
  * Fixed up the Creature Warning to be on screen for a shorter time, changed its position and made it not show up again until you’ve left a previous combat encounter



## Balancing

  * Reduced the size of the radius for Factory buildings preventing creatures from respawning
  * Increased the individual [Cluster Nobelisk](/wiki/Cluster_Nobelisk "Cluster Nobelisk") Explosion damage to 40 (was 30 before)
  * Reduced [Xeno Basher](/wiki/Xeno_Basher "Xeno Basher") damage from 10 to 9
  * Increased the [Flying Crab](/wiki/Flying_Crab "Flying Crab") sphere radius to 60 (Was 32 before)
