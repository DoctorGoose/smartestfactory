Template for easier use of [Module:FormatNumber](/wiki/Module:FormatNumber "Module:FormatNumber"), which adds thousands separator to big numbers. 

Usage

`{{FormatNumber|8008135}}` gets you `8 008 135`

Also works with negative numbers and fractional parts: 

`{{FormatNumber|-123456.789}}` gets you `-123 456.789`
