# The HUB/FICSIT Tips

Each time the Pioneer respawns in the [HUB Living Quarters](/wiki/The_HUB#Living_quarters "The HUB"), while they wait to respawn, the Pioneer gets a chance to see a "tip" from [FICSIT Incorporated](/wiki/FICSIT_Inc. "FICSIT Inc.") how they can do better.   
  
Below the "tip" the Pioneer will see INITIALIZING RESPAWN_ SEQUENCE. BAT..., followed by a "progress bar" showing ▋▋▋▋▋moving left to right, followed by some random statements that may or may not be related to the "tip" shown.
