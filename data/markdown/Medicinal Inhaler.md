## Medicinal Inhaler

[ ](/wiki/File:Medicinal_Inhaler.png "Medicinal Inhaler.png")

_Fully restores health when used.  
The FICSIT Medicinal Inhaler can be produced from a variety of different ingredients._

[MAM Alien Megafauna Research](/wiki/Alien_Megafauna_Research "Alien Megafauna Research") \- Protein Inhaler OR

## Item

### Stack size

50

### [Sink points](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink")

125

## Equipment

### Equipment slot

Hand

The **Medicinal Inhaler** is a consumable healing item that restores the [pioneer](/wiki/Pioneer "Pioneer") to full [health](/wiki/Health "Health"). It can only be crafted at the [Equipment Workshop](/wiki/Equipment_Workshop "Equipment Workshop"). All of its recipes are unlocked in the [MAM](/wiki/MAM "MAM").

## Usage

### AWESOME Sink

**[](/wiki/Medicinal_Inhaler "Medicinal Inhaler")Medicinal Inhaler** can be sunk into the [](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") [AWESOME Sink](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") to yield **125 points**.

## Trivia

  * The Medicinal Inhaler heals more health segments compared to its ingredients total healing power: 100 vs 65 (default recipe). However, it can be risky if you wait until the health almost depletes before consuming the inhaler.
  * Using an Inhaler requires a relatively long animation, and as a result, the time it takes to use the Inhaler and for the animation to complete (and therefore to receive healing) can leave enough time for the player to die anyway from enough damage. In this case, once the animation would otherwise end, the player's health will refill and the inhaler will be used, despite not preventing the player's death.
