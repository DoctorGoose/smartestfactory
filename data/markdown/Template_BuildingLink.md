## Usage

Blank Template 
    
    
    {{BuildingLink|"Building Name"|"Alt title"|size="Insert Desired Size"(Optional)}}

1\. 
    
    
    {{BuildingLink|Miner Mk.1|size=128}}

2\. 
    
    
    {{BuildingLink|Miner Mk.1|size=64}}

3\. 
    
    
    {{BuildingLink|Miner Mk.1}}

4\. 
    
    
    {{BuildingLink|Miner Mk.1|Tiny Miner|size=16}}
