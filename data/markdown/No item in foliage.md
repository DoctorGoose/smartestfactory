# No item in foliage

## No item in foliage  
  
[ ](/wiki/File:No_item_in_foliage.png "No item in foliage.png")

**No item in foliage** appears when aiming at foliage or rocks that yield no item. Trying to pick it up makes it simply disappear. 

## Contents

  * 1 Occurrence
  * 2 References
  * 3 Trivia
  * 4 See also



## Occurrence

  * [Crude Oil](/wiki/Crude_Oil "Crude Oil") blobs in the [Southern Forest](/wiki/Southern_Forest "Southern Forest")
    * Used to yield Crude Oil barrels while it was an item prior to [Patch 0.3](/wiki/Patch_0.3 "Patch 0.3")[1] alongside Crude Oil bearing plants
  * Some medium-sized rocks in the [Dune Desert](/wiki/Dune_Desert "Dune Desert")
    * As of [Patch 0.4.0.0](/wiki/Patch_0.4.0.0 "Patch 0.4.0.0")
  * Various rocks in [caves](/wiki/Cave "Cave")
    * As of [Patch 0.5.0.0](/wiki/Patch_0.5.0.0 "Patch 0.5.0.0") and more as of [Patch 0.6.0.0](/wiki/Patch_0.6.0.0 "Patch 0.6.0.0")

## Trivia

  * The reflection in the shown [Crude Oil](/wiki/Crude_Oil "Crude Oil") blob is HDRI_Epic_Courtyard_Daylight, a default Unreal Engine texture cube used in reflective objects most of the time, unless lumen is on 
    * The same texture is used in the [Pioneer](/wiki/Pioneer "Pioneer")'s helmet reflection
