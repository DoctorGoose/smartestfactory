Used to display narrative spoilers. 

## Usage

Intended to be used within Narrative sections of pages, per the [style guide](/wiki/Satisfactory_Wiki:Style_guide "Satisfactory Wiki:Style guide"). 

### Parameters

  * 1 - Required. This is the displayed text.



## Example

`{{Spoilerbox|Lizard Doggos are cruel.}}`

**Expand to reveal spoiler**

Lizard Doggos are cruel.
