`{{citation needed}}`

  * Tells a visitors that the statement is not supported by any form of given evidence.
  * Displays: [_[citation needed](/wiki/Category:Citation_needed "Category:Citation needed")_]
  * If you see citation needed somewhere, try to prove it (or to prove it's wrong)
  * A citation needed should be fixed as soon as possible
  * See also [Citation needed on Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Citation_needed "wikipedia:Citation needed")



**Do not add this template onto your own contributions.** If you add something but can't provide valid source along with it, then don't add it at all. 

Any pages using this template will be automatically placed in the [Citation needed](/wiki/Category:Citation_needed "Category:Citation needed") category.
