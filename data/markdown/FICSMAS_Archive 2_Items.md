## Obtaining

### Unlocking

Most items are unlocked via the [FICSMAS Holiday Event Research chain](/wiki/FICSMAS#MAM_Research "FICSMAS") in the [](/wiki/MAM "MAM") [MAM](/wiki/MAM "MAM"). 

Some items are found only in the [FICSMAS Calendar](/wiki/FICSMAS#FICSMAS_Calendar "FICSMAS"), which limits progress based on how many days have passed in the event so far. 

### FICSMAS Gifts

FICSMAS Gifts can be obtained from falling [Giant FICSMAS Gifts](/wiki/FICSMAS#Giant_FICSMAS_Gifts "FICSMAS"). 

FICSMAS Gift Trees, once unlocked, can be used to automate their production, at a rate of 15/min. [Conveyor Belts](/wiki/Conveyor_Belts "Conveyor Belts") are required to bring out the produced gifts, as the trees themselves cannot not be interacted with.
