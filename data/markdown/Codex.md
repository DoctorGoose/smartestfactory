# Codex

The **Codex** window functions as the in-game encyclopedia. Not all the information is displayed from the beginning; instead, it only displays information for items that have been discovered by the player. It contains three tabs: Inbox, Tutorials, and Recipes. 

## Contents

  * 1 Inbox
  * 2 Tutorial
  * 3 Recipes
  * 4 Narrative
  * 5 Gallery
  * 6 History



## Inbox

The inbox tab contains a log of messages from [ADA](/wiki/ADA "ADA") and [???](/wiki/%3F%3F%3F "???"), such as discoveries, [milestone](/wiki/Milestones "Milestones") and research completion, etc. Clicking the 'play' button will playback the voice message from ADA and/or ???. 

## Tutorial

The tutorial tab contains a log of tutorial messages from ADA, such as from dismantling the [drop pod](/wiki/Drop-pod "Drop-pod"), building [the HUB](/wiki/The_HUB "The HUB"), etc. Clicking the 'play' button will playback the voice message from ADA. 

## Recipes

The recipes tab can be used to view crafting recipes of all currently craftable/buildable items. Only items that can be currently crafted/built are shown (e.g. the recipe for [Computers](/wiki/Computer "Computer") is only shown after unlocking the [Tier 5](/wiki/Tier_5 "Tier 5") milestone: Industrial Manufacturing). 

It has three sections, Parts, Equipment, and Buildings, showing recipes from the [Craft Bench](/wiki/Craft_Bench "Craft Bench"), [Equipment Workshop](/wiki/Equipment_Workshop "Equipment Workshop") and [Build Gun](/wiki/Build_Gun "Build Gun") respectively, except for [Vehicles](/wiki/Vehicles "Vehicles"), which are shown in the Parts section. 

All recipes can now be expanded to show the per-minute stats of all ingredients and products. You can also simulate overclocking in there now and see how that affects the recipes requirements and output. 

## Narrative

When performing specific actions, the player will hear [ADA](/wiki/ADA "ADA") speak, sometimes delivering a corrupted message in a different voice shown as [???](/wiki/%3F%3F%3F "???"), both of which are recorded in the Codex.
