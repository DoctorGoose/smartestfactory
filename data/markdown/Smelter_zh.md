# Smelter/zh

## Smelter  
  
[ ](/wiki/File:Smelter.png "Smelter.png")

_Smelts ore into ingots.  
Can be automated by feeding ore in via a Conveyor Belt connected to the input port. The resulting ingots can be automatically extracted by connecting a Conveyor Belt to the output port._

## 超頻

_Main article:_[Clock speed](/wiki/Clock_speed "Clock speed")

The Smelter/zh can be overclocked using [Power Shards](/wiki/Power_Shard "Power Shard"). Overclocking increases the input/output speed of the Smelter/zh at the cost of exponentially increased [power](/wiki/Power "Power") demand. 

Clock speed | 25% | 50% | 75% | 100% | 150% | 200% | 250%   
---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---  
Consumption (MW)  | 0.64 | 1.6 | 2.73 | 4 | 6.84 | 10 | 13.43   
  
## 提示

  * 所有冶煉機的配方在[精煉廠](/wiki/Refinery "Refinery")中都有更有效的替代品，這些替代品可以作為[替代配方](/wiki/Alternate_recipes "Alternate recipes")獲得。在遊戲後期，冶煉機可以完全由精煉廠取代，代價是需要更多的空間和消耗更多的電力，但極大地提高了錠的產量（鋁除外）。

  * [Patch 0.3.4.6](/wiki/Patch_0.3.4.6 "Patch 0.3.4.6"): 修正了冶煉機和鑄造廠的材料應用錯誤

  * [Patch 0.3](/wiki/Patch_0.3 "Patch 0.3"): 調整了鑄造廠、製造機和冶煉機的間隙
  * [Patch 0.1.10](/wiki/Patch_0.1.10 "Patch 0.1.10"): 重新調整了鑄造廠和冶煉機的輸入，使連接更緊密
  * [Patch 0.1.9](/wiki/Patch_0.1.9 "Patch 0.1.9"): 調整了鑄造廠、燃煤發電機、燃料發電機、石油精煉廠、製造機、冶煉機和組裝機的輸入/輸出對齊
  * [Patch 0.1.5](/wiki/Patch_0.1.5 "Patch 0.1.5"): 調整了冶煉機、製造機和組裝機的間隙
  * [Patch Closed Alpha 3](/wiki/Patch_Closed_Alpha_3 "Patch Closed Alpha 3"): 實施了重要性管理器，並增加了對冶煉機和製造機的支持
  * [Patch Closed Alpha 2](/wiki/Patch_Closed_Alpha_2 "Patch Closed Alpha 2"): 建築聲音：冶煉機和[採礦機](/wiki/Miner "Miner")在閒置時將不再動畫和發出聲音。
