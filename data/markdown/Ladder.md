## Construction

The Ladder's height can be adjusted between two and 20 meters in steps of two meters: 2/4/6 … 18/20 meters. When building, the first click sets its starting position, then looking up or down sets the height, and the second click confirms the construction. 

The handle at the top extends one meter higher than the climbable height. 

Additional Ladder segments can be attached each to other, snapping to either the top or the bottom, regardless of whether the ladder is free-standing or not. This makes it a very versatile tool for vertical traversal. 

When the “Build in One Click” [setting](/wiki/Settings#Gameplay "Settings") is enabled, Ladders can be built using one [](/wiki/File:Keyboard_White_Mouse_Left.png "Left") click. 

### Diving

A ladder penetrating water surface, such as the sea level, can be used for diving into the water. The downward [`S`](/wiki/Controls "Controls") key needs to be pressed very slowly to avoid being washed away. While in water, the player must remain attached to the ladder or they will begin floating to the surface. 

This method can be used to obtain some [collectibles](/wiki/Collectibles "Collectibles") located underwater.
