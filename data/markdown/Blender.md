The **Blender** is a late-game building used for a wide variety of late-game recipes involving both fluids and solid resources. 

The color of the fluid in the blending chamber changes depending on the recipe chosen. For example, [Cooling System](/wiki/Cooling_System "Cooling System") makes the fluid blue.

## Overclocking

_Main article:_[Clock speed](/wiki/Clock_speed "Clock speed")

The Blender can be overclocked using [Power Shards](/wiki/Power_Shard "Power Shard"). Overclocking increases the input/output speed of the Blender at the cost of exponentially increased [power](/wiki/Power "Power") demand. 

Clock speed | 25% | 50% | 75% | 100% | 150% | 200% | 250%   
---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---  
Consumption (MW)  | 12 | 30 | 51.27 | 75 | 128.19 | 187.5 | 251.83   
  
## Byproducts

The Blender stops working if either of its outputs backs up, similar to a [Refinery](/wiki/Refinery "Refinery"). To keep the Blender running, **All** its products must be constantly removed from it. 

## Head lift

_Main article:_[Head lift](/wiki/Head_lift "Head lift")

The Blender has a recommended [head lift](/wiki/Head_lift "Head lift") of 10 meters, measured from the center of its Pipeline output. 

## Tips

  * When using the Diluted Fuel alternate recipe, a convenient ratio would be five [Heavy Oil Residue](/wiki/Heavy_Oil_Residue "Heavy Oil Residue") [Refineries](/wiki/Refinery "Refinery") to four Diluted Fuel Blenders. Each minute this will consume 150 [Crude Oil](/wiki/Crude_Oil "Crude Oil") and produce 400 [Fuel](/wiki/Fuel "Fuel") and 100 [Polymer Resin](/wiki/Polymer_Resin "Polymer Resin"). The resulting Polymer Resin neatly satisfies one [Residual Rubber](/wiki/Residual_Rubber "Residual Rubber") Refinery and one [Residual Plastic](/wiki/Residual_Plastic "Residual Plastic") Refinery, resulting in 20 Rubber and 20 Plastic each minute. The total water utilized by this system will require four [Water Extractors](/wiki/Water_Extractor "Water Extractor") with one underclocked by 83.3% to output 100/m³.
