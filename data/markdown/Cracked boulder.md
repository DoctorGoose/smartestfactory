# Cracked boulder

## Cracked boulder  
  
[ ](/wiki/File:Boulder_Caterium.png "Boulder Caterium.png")

_Creates obstructions. Can be detonated._

**Cracked boulders** are large destructible stones that appear in the [world](/wiki/World "World") solely to create obstructions. They can be blown up with [](/wiki/Nobelisk "Nobelisk") [Nobelisks](/wiki/Nobelisk "Nobelisk") or [](/wiki/Explosive_Rebar "Explosive Rebar") [Explosive Rebars](/wiki/Explosive_Rebar "Explosive Rebar"). Once destroyed, they will never re-appear.

## Occurrence

Cracked boulders can appear: 

  * On [Resource Nodes](/wiki/Resource_Node "Resource Node"), to block the construction of [Miner](/wiki/Miner "Miner") buildings.
  * Covering up [collectibles](/wiki/Collectibles "Collectibles"), such as [Power Slugs](/wiki/Power_Slug "Power Slug").
  * Blocking a path, usually at the borders between [biomes](/wiki/Biomes "Biomes").
  * Blocking a [cave](/wiki/Cave "Cave") entrance.



## Method of Destruction

You can use the following to destroy them: 

  * [Nobelisk](/wiki/Nobelisk "Nobelisk")
  * [Explosive Rebar](/wiki/Explosive_Rebar "Explosive Rebar")



## Trivia

  * There are 689 total cracked boulders in the world.
  * Before Nobelisks were introduced to the game, it was not possible to remove cracked boulders, rendering some Resource Nodes obstructed or areas inaccessible.
