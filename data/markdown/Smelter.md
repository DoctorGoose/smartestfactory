# Smelter

## Smelter  
  
[ ](/wiki/File:Smelter.png "Smelter.png")

_Smelts ore into ingots.  
Can be automated by feeding ore in via a Conveyor Belt connected to the input port. The resulting ingots can be automatically extracted by connecting a Conveyor Belt to the output port._

## Overclocking

_Main article:_[Clock speed](/wiki/Clock_speed "Clock speed")

The Smelter can be overclocked using [Power Shards](/wiki/Power_Shard "Power Shard"). Overclocking increases the input/output speed of the Smelter at the cost of exponentially increased [power](/wiki/Power "Power") demand. 

Clock speed | 25% | 50% | 75% | 100% | 150% | 200% | 250%   
---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---  
Consumption (MW)  | 0.64 | 1.6 | 2.73 | 4 | 6.84 | 10 | 13.43   
  
## Tips

  * All Smelter recipes have more effective alternatives in [Refineries](/wiki/Refineries "Refineries"), obtained as [alternate recipes](/wiki/Alternate_recipes "Alternate recipes"). In the late game, Smelters can be completely replaced by Refineries, at the cost of requiring a bit more space and drawing more power, but greatly increasing ingot yield (except for Aluminum).

## Early game development

Prior to Closed Alpha, In early game development there was not a dedicated smelter, but that function was combined in a multi-purpose Manufacturing Plant. In Sprint 6 (week 12) smelters become its own buildable.
