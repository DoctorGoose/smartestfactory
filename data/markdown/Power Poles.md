**Power Poles** are small towers that can be used to extend the range of [Power Lines](/wiki/Power_Line "Power Line") and to transfer [power](/wiki/Power "Power") from buildings that produce power to buildings that consume it. Power Poles can be placed on the regular ground but will also snap to [foundations](/wiki/Foundations "Foundations"). 

Higher tiers of Power Poles are unlocked in the [Caterium Research](/wiki/Caterium_Research "Caterium Research") tree. After a tier of Power Pole is researched, the corresponding [Wall Outlet](/wiki/Wall_Outlet "Wall Outlet") is made available for purchase in the AWESOME Shop. 

Each Power Pole can handle an unlimited amount of power passing through it, however, they have a limited number of connections possible based on the tier of the power pole. Power Poles can be linked together with a [Power Line](/wiki/Power_Line "Power Line") for up to 100 meters apart. It is recommended to leave at least one spare connection on each Pole for lines coming from and going to other poles.

## Construction

  * While constructing Power Lines and the 2nd target is not a building, an additional Power Pole will be automatically placed on the cursor's location, as displayed by the hologram. 
    * The mark of the Power Pole is copied (if the first target is a Mk.2 Power Pole, another Mk.2 Power Pole will be placed); building from other targets (such as building connectors or [Power Towers](/wiki/Power_Tower "Power Tower")) defaults to Mk.1 Power Poles.
    * This hologram will convert to a Wall Outlet if the cursor is aimed at either a wall or the bottom/side of a foundation. 
      * Additionally, when placing Wall Outlets, the resulting outlet will be either single or double depending on the first target.
  * Power Poles can be placed on a Power Line between two other Power Poles by aiming at the Line.
  * Power Poles can be upgraded in-place just like Conveyors and Miners.
  * Holding [`Ctrl`](/wiki/Controls "Controls") when building a Power Line off another connector aligns the new Power Pole to the first target
