## Usage

Place in plain text or in places where plain text is seen. Can be also used in infoboxes. Generally should be positioned in () brackets. 

Do not use this in History sections of pages and avoid marking whole sentences or lots of words. 

### Parameters

  * 1 - Required. This is the displayed text.



## Example
