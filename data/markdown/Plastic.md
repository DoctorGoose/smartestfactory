### AWESOME Sink

**[](/wiki/Plastic "Plastic")Plastic** can be sunk into the [](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") [AWESOME Sink](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") to yield **75 points**.

## Tips

Later in the game when you have collected the majority of the [Hard Drives](/wiki/Hard_Drive "Hard Drive"), be sure to unlock the following [Alternate Recipes](/wiki/Alternate_Recipes "Alternate Recipes"): 

  * [](/wiki/Heavy_Oil_Residue "Heavy Oil Residue") [Heavy Oil Residue](/wiki/Heavy_Oil_Residue "Heavy Oil Residue")
  * [](/wiki/Packaged_Fuel "Packaged Fuel") [Diluted Packaged Fuel](/wiki/Packaged_Fuel "Packaged Fuel") or [](/wiki/Fuel "Fuel") [Diluted Fuel](/wiki/Fuel "Fuel")
  * [](/wiki/Plastic "Plastic") Recycled Plastic
  * [](/wiki/Rubber "Rubber") [Recycled Rubber](/wiki/Rubber "Rubber")



Combine these with [Packaged Water](/wiki/Packaged_Water "Packaged Water"), Unpackaged [Fuel](/wiki/Fuel "Fuel"), and Residual [Rubber](/wiki/Rubber "Rubber") to form a closed-loop production of Plastic and/or Rubber without any wastage, increasing the Oil to Plastic/Rubber conversion ratio up to 1:3. [Empty Canisters](/wiki/Empty_Canister "Empty Canister") from fuel unpackaging are reused and recycled back to the water packaging.
