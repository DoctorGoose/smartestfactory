Infobox building is an automatically generated infobox that sources its data from Docs.json. Its item counterpart is `{{[Infobox item](/wiki/Template:Infobox_item "Template:Infobox item")}}`. 

## Parameters

The `className` argument is the only mandatory argument. A number of vehicle and size-related parameters can also be supplied, as they cannot be acquired from Docs.json. 

Parameter | Usage   
---|---  
**className** | **className of the building (mandatory)**  
overclockable | Whether the building is overclockable (boolean)   
inventorySize | Inventory size (in slots or m3)   
beltInputs | Conveyor inputs   
beltOutputs | Conveyor outputs   
pipeInputs | Pipeline inputs   
pipeOutputs | Pipeline outputs   
maxSpeed | Maximum speed (measured)   
time0_50 | Time to reach 50 km/h (measured)   
size_width | Width   
size_height | Height   
size_length | Length   
size_note | Additional note, such as stacking behavior
