# Gas Pillar

## Gas Pillar

[ ](/wiki/File:PoisonPillars1.jpg "PoisonPillars1.jpg")

_A pillar producing poisonous gas._

## Environment

### Behavior

Hostile, stationary

**Gas Pillars** are rocky, hole-ridden outcrops found throughout the world that emit [Poison Gas](/wiki/Poison_Gas "Poison Gas") around themselves. Gas Pillars are found in groups.

## Behavior

Gas Pillars emit a steady cloud of [Poison Gas](/wiki/Poison_Gas "Poison Gas"). In some areas, the gas may extend outward further than where the Gas Pillars are located. 

Gas Pillars are typically found around various collectibles and exploration rewards that can be found around the map: [Somersloops](/wiki/Somersloop "Somersloop"), [Mercer Spheres](/wiki/Mercer_Sphere "Mercer Sphere"), [Power Slugs](/wiki/Power_Slug "Power Slug"), and [Crash Sites](/wiki/Crash_Site "Crash Site"). 

### Combat

Gas Pillars are immune to most forms of weaponry but can be destroyed by the explosions from [Nobelisks](/wiki/Nobelisk_Detonator "Nobelisk Detonator"), [Cluster Nobelisks](/wiki/Cluster_Nobelisk "Cluster Nobelisk"), [Nuke Nobelisks](/wiki/Nuke_Nobelisk "Nuke Nobelisk"), and [Explosive Rebars](/wiki/Explosive_Rebar "Explosive Rebar"). Once destroyed, Gas Pillars do not respawn. 

A [Gas Mask](/wiki/Gas_Mask "Gas Mask") combined with [Gas filter](/wiki/Gas_Filter "Gas Filter") is necessary to navigate in Poison Gas to prevent taking damage. 

## Tips

  * Small structures, such as Power Poles or Conveyor Poles, can be built on top of Gas Pillars.
  * A [Hover Pack](/wiki/Hover_Pack "Hover Pack") generally allows Pioneer to fly over the Gas Pillars with ease, sometimes even collecting loot from a safe distance thanks to the interactive range extension when flying with a Hover Pack.
  * Vehicles can be used to explore past areas that are cut off by gas, as the player takes no damage from anything whilst in a vehicle.

  * [Patch 1.0.0.5](/wiki/Patch_1.0.0.5 "Patch 1.0.0.5"): All of the remaining smoke effect lingering from Gas Pillars should now be fixed
  * [Patch 1.0.0.4](/wiki/Patch_1.0.0.4 "Patch 1.0.0.4")
    * Fixed [Nobelisks](/wiki/Nobelisk "Nobelisk") getting stuck in the air around the area of a recently destroyed Gas Pillar or [Destructible Rock](/wiki/Cracked_boulder "Cracked boulder")
    * Fixed Gas from Gas Pillars not being removed in some areas even after destroying the Gas Pillars
  * [Patch 1.0.0.3](/wiki/Patch_1.0.0.3 "Patch 1.0.0.3"): Fixed objects inside the poison clouds of Gas Pillars being uninteractable
  * [Patch 1.0](/wiki/Patch_1.0 "Patch 1.0"): (undocumented) Gas no longer damages the player while they are in a vehicle.
  * [Patch 0.8.3.1](/wiki/Patch_0.8.3.1 "Patch 0.8.3.1"): Fixed a bug where it was sometimes impossible to hand mine near Gas Pillars
  * [Patch 0.8.2.7](/wiki/Patch_0.8.2.7 "Patch 0.8.2.7"): Fixed some log spam related to Gas Pillars
  * [Patch 0.8.0.3](/wiki/Patch_0.8.0.3 "Patch 0.8.0.3"): Fixed some issues with the Gas Pillar effects in a few areas of Dune Desert and Spire Coast
  * [Patch 0.8.0.0](/wiki/Patch_0.8.0.0 "Patch 0.8.0.0"): Now can be permanently destroyed from Nobelisk explosions
  * [Patch 0.3.2.0](/wiki/Patch_0.3.2.0 "Patch 0.3.2.0"): Poison Gas around Pillars should now be visible again
  * [Patch 0.3](/wiki/Patch_0.3 "Patch 0.3"): Poison Gas around Pillars is no longer visible (bug)
