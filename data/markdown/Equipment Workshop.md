## Ingre­dients

**6 ×[](/wiki/Iron_Plate "Iron Plate") [Iron Plate](/wiki/Iron_Plate "Iron Plate")**

**4 ×[](/wiki/Iron_Rod "Iron Rod") [Iron Rod](/wiki/Iron_Rod "Iron Rod")**

The **Equipment Workshop** is the station at which engineers can manually craft non-crafting components such as tools, ammunition and consumables. 

Crafting is done by holding [](/wiki/File:Keyboard_White_Mouse_Left.png "Left") on the Craft button. To craft continuously, [`Space`](/wiki/Controls "Controls") can be tapped to hold the Craft button. 

The workshop has a large front opening, and a small doorway at the back. The front opening is about the size of a wall, meaning that the workshop can be integrated into a larger building.
