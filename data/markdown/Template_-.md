# Template:-

[](/wiki/File:Template-info.svg) Documentation

[[purge](https://satisfactory.wiki.gg/wiki/Template:-?action=purge)]

This template allows you to clear the left side, the right side, or both 

To clear the left, simply put `{{-|left}}`. To clear the right, put `{{-|right}}`. To clear both sides, put `{{-}}`.
