# FICSMAS/Archive 2/Equipment

_This page is an archive of past versions of the current page._  
  
_The contents are kept for reference purposes only, and should be preserved in their current form. Do not add new comments here or edit this page._

During [FICSMAS](/wiki/FICSMAS "FICSMAS"), unique **equipment** can be crafted. Currently, this is only the Candy Cane Basher. Previously, Snowball Mittens were also available, the functionality of which was replaced by supporting different ammo types in the [Nobelisk Detonator](/wiki/Nobelisk_Detonator "Nobelisk Detonator").

[ ](/wiki/File:FICSMAS_2021_Candy_Cane_Basher.png "FICSMAS 2021 Candy Cane Basher.png")

_A heavy, delicious self-defense weapon for use at melee range._

The **Candy Cane Basher** is the FICSMAS variant of the [](/wiki/Xeno-Basher "Xeno-Basher") [Xeno-Basher](/wiki/Xeno-Basher "Xeno-Basher"). Both weapons are functionally identical, other than in appearance. 

### Unlocking

The Candy Cane Basher is unlocked via the FICSMAS Research chain in the [](/wiki/MAM "MAM") [MAM](/wiki/MAM "MAM").

## Snowball Pile

[ ](/wiki/File:FICSMAS_2021_Snowball_Pile.png "FICSMAS 2021 Snowball Pile.png")

_Disclaimer = Snowballs in pile not included in ammo reserve._

### Class name

`BP_EquipmentDescriptorSnowballPile_C`

## Item

### [Stack size](/wiki/Stack "Stack")

1 

## Equipment

### Equipment slot

Hands

### Ammo

Snowball

The **Snowball Pile** was the FICSMAS variant of the [](/wiki/Nobelisk_Detonator "Nobelisk Detonator") [Nobelisk Detonator](/wiki/Nobelisk_Detonator "Nobelisk Detonator") and used [](https://satisfactory.wiki.gg/wiki/FICSMAS/Archive_2/Items) [Snowballs](/wiki/FICSMAS/Archive_2/Items "FICSMAS/Archive 2/Items") as ammo. 

As of [Patch 0.5.1.3](/wiki/Patch_0.5.1.3#Quality_of_life "Patch 0.5.1.3"), Snowballs can be loaded into the Nobelisk Detonator itself. 

### Crafting

The Snowball Pile could be crafted using 100× [](https://satisfactory.wiki.gg/wiki/FICSMAS/Archive_2/Items) [Actual Snow](/wiki/FICSMAS/Archive_2/Items "FICSMAS/Archive 2/Items"), 20× [](https://satisfactory.wiki.gg/wiki/FICSMAS/Archive_2/Items) [Candy Cane](/wiki/FICSMAS/Archive_2/Items "FICSMAS/Archive 2/Items") and 10× [](https://satisfactory.wiki.gg/wiki/FICSMAS/Archive_2/Items) [FICSMAS Bow](/wiki/FICSMAS/Archive_2/Items "FICSMAS/Archive 2/Items").
