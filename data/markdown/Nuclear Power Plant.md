The **Nuclear Power Plant** is a [power generator](/wiki/Power_generator "Power generator") [building](/wiki/Building "Building") that generates [power](/wiki/Power "Power") by burning [](/wiki/Uranium_Fuel_Rod "Uranium Fuel Rod") [Uranium Fuel Rods](/wiki/Uranium_Fuel_Rod "Uranium Fuel Rod"), [](/wiki/Plutonium_Fuel_Rod "Plutonium Fuel Rod") [Plutonium Fuel Rods](/wiki/Plutonium_Fuel_Rod "Plutonium Fuel Rod") or [](/wiki/Ficsonium_Fuel_Rod "Ficsonium Fuel Rod") [Ficsonium Fuel Rods](/wiki/Ficsonium_Fuel_Rod "Ficsonium Fuel Rod"). The former two produce [](/wiki/Uranium_Waste "Uranium Waste") [Uranium Waste](/wiki/Uranium_Waste "Uranium Waste") or [](/wiki/Plutonium_Waste "Plutonium Waste") [Plutonium Waste](/wiki/Plutonium_Waste "Plutonium Waste") respectively. 

One Nuclear Power Plant produces 2,500 MW at 100% [clock speed](/wiki/Clock_speed "Clock speed").

## Fuel energy values

At 100% clock speed, one Nuclear Power Plant consumes 240 m3 [Water](/wiki/Water "Water")/min, no matter which type of fuel rod is used. 

When [overclocked](/wiki/Overclock "Overclock"), Nuclear Power Plants produce more power linearly. Therefore, at 250% clock speed, they operate 2.5 times as fast, for resource input, waste output, and power production.

## Trivia

  * The old version shown in its [teaser video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C1CbfQ_pNG4) was an air-cooled design with a completely exposed core. The fuel rods were operated by a robotic arm, which could occasionally drop the fuel rod into the cooling liquid under the reactor. The belt input was located at the front and output at the back rather than right next to each other.
  * Before its official implementation, it was revealed that the design of the Nuclear Reactor/Power Plant had been changed.[1]
  * When building on foundations, it is possible to remove five foundations at the center of its footprint, and yet the Nuclear Power Plant can still be built on it without getting the 'Floor is too steep' warning, as long as each of its supports lands on a foundation.
  * The Nuclear Power Plant has a faint steam animation rising from the top of its tower. This steam animation occurs when the Power Plant has fuel, and will even occur when the Power Plant has no water.
  * The Nuclear Power Plant will show [Cherenkov Radiation](https://www.energy.gov/ne/articles/cherenkov-radiation-explained) in form of a blue circle on [Foundations](/wiki/Foundations "Foundations") upon which the Nuclear Power Plant is placed.
  * The Robot arm replacing the fuel rods in the Nuclear Power Plant can play one of two possible animations. One where it pulls out and replaces the rod without problems and one where it struggles to pull out the rod and uses force to do so before also pushing the new rod in with force.
