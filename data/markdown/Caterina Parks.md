Caterina Parks

**Caterina Parks** is a CEO of [FICSIT Inc.](/wiki/FICSIT_Inc. "FICSIT Inc."). 

Previously, Caterina was [Steve](/wiki/Steve "Steve")'s boss in Satisfactory's lore. She was middle-aged and has been described as "powerful" and "intimidating"[1]. However, Caterina and Steve have been officially retconned from Satisfactory's story as of 16th of August 2024[2]. 

Caterina is still a canonical CEO of FICSIT, although not part of the story as originally intended (as seen in one of [ADA](/wiki/ADA "ADA")'s voice lines). The voice line is worded in such a way that it is ambiguous if she is a current or former CEO, although the latter is more likely. 

Despite her removal from the story, [Caterium](/wiki/Caterium_Ore "Caterium Ore") is named after her, with [ADA](/wiki/ADA "ADA") describing her as "FICSIT's greatest CEO".[3]

## Former Speculation

  * The female voice in the game file _0926.wav_ saying _"Huh, must have been a dream."_ possibly belonged to her. The sound is currently unused in the game, as Caterina is no longer part of Satisfactory's cast. It should be noted that a male voice (possibly Steve's) said the exact same thing in the file _0379.wav_.
