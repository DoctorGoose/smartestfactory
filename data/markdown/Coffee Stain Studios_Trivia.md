# Coffee Stain Studios/Trivia

This page serves as a rundown of various trivia on [Coffee Stain Studios](/wiki/Coffee_Stain_Studios "Coffee Stain Studios") employees, mostly from one-off mentions in the company's YouTube videos or on Dev Streams. 

## General

  * If something goes wrong, blame [Simon](/wiki/Simon "Simon").[trivia 1][trivia 2]
  * Episode 1 of [Snutt's](/wiki/Snutt "Snutt") interview series features [Jace](/wiki/Jace "Jace").[trivia 3]
  * Snutt was hired on the spot after an interview obtained through a referral from Jace.[trivia 4]
  * Coffee Stainers are allowed to spend a day every two weeks working on creative projects that are not necessarily for the game, so long as there's some benefit for Coffee Stain.[trivia 5]
