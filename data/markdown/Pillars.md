# Pillars

**Pillars** are a set of decorative [buildings](/wiki/Buildings "Buildings"). They attach to [Foundations](/wiki/Foundations "Foundations"), [Walkways](/wiki/Walkways "Walkways") and [Railways](/wiki/Railway "Railway").

## Unlocking

Pillars are unlocked in three sets from the [AWESOME Shop](/wiki/AWESOME_Shop "AWESOME Shop"). Each set unlocks the given Big and Small Pillar variant, as well as the Big and Small Pillar Support, for 2 [](/wiki/FICSIT_Coupon "FICSIT Coupon") [Coupons](/wiki/FICSIT_Coupon "FICSIT Coupon").

## Construction

  * Pillars can be built one at a time or 10 at once using the [Zoop](/wiki/Build_Gun#Zoop "Build Gun") construction mode.



## History

  * [Patch 1.0.1.1](/wiki/Patch_1.0.1.1 "Patch 1.0.1.1"): Reverted the snapping changes when building on the sides of Pillars to allow free placement again
  * [Patch 0.8.2.4](/wiki/Patch_0.8.2.4 "Patch 0.8.2.4"): Fixed colouring on Pillars not working
  * [Patch 0.8.2.2](/wiki/Patch_0.8.2.2 "Patch 0.8.2.2"): Removed restrictions for snapping [Signs](/wiki/Signs "Signs") to Pillars, they can now snap to any side at any time
  * [Patch 0.8.2.0](/wiki/Patch_0.8.2.0 "Patch 0.8.2.0"): Improved Beam Support to Pillar snapping
  * [Patch 0.7.0.5](/wiki/Patch_0.7.0.5 "Patch 0.7.0.5"): Pillars now affect Blueprint size
  * [Patch 0.7.0.2](/wiki/Patch_0.7.0.2 "Patch 0.7.0.2"): Fixed wonky Pillar to Pillar snapping
  * [Patch 0.6.1.2](/wiki/Patch_0.6.1.2 "Patch 0.6.1.2"): Fixed a bug where snapping a pillar to another pillar would sometimes move it in weird directions
  * [Patch 0.5.0.13](/wiki/Patch_0.5.0.13 "Patch 0.5.0.13"): Fixed up LOD bias on Pillars
  * [Patch 0.5.0.9](/wiki/Patch_0.5.0.9 "Patch 0.5.0.9")
    * Fixed issues with Pillar snapping
    * Fixed [Signs](/wiki/Signs "Signs") being able to be placed on weird rotations on Pillars
  * [Patch 0.5.0.8](/wiki/Patch_0.5.0.8 "Patch 0.5.0.8"): [Signs](/wiki/Signs "Signs") can now snap to [Beams](/wiki/Beams "Beams") and Pillars
  * [Patch 0.5.0.0](/wiki/Patch_0.5.0.0 "Patch 0.5.0.0"): 
    * United Pillar Top and Pillar Base into a single Big Pillar Support that rotates
    * Introduced Concrete and Frame Big Pillar material variants
    * Introduced Small Pillars and Small Pillar Support
    * Now can be placed freely on foundation grids rather than being constrained to foundation centers, as well as on the sides of foundations
    * Big Metal Pillar now costs 3 Concrete and 2 [Iron Plates](/wiki/Iron_Plate "Iron Plate") instead of 6 Concrete
    * Big Pillar Support now costs 5 Concrete instead of 6
  * [Patch 0.3](/wiki/Patch_0.3 "Patch 0.3"): Introduced Pillar Top, Pillar Middle and Pillar Base
