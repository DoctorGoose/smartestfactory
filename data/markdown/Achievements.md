# Achievements

**Achievements** are awarded by performing certain actions or via game progression. They are available on both Steam and the Epic Games Store.[1][2][3]

## Awarding

Achievements are awarded by triggering certain actions, or by [Milestone](/wiki/Milestone "Milestone"), [MAM](/wiki/MAM "MAM"), or [Space Elevator Project Assembly Phase](/wiki/Space_Elevator "Space Elevator") progression. All achievements can be unlocked within a single save file.[4]

You can not retroactively be awarded an achievement by loading an older game save, even if the requirements for a specific achievement were completed before.[5]

  * Achievements related to some action can be awarded by doing the action again.
  * Achievements related to progression can only be awarded if _that progression_ had not already been completed, or by starting a new game save.[6]



The use of [Advanced Game Settings](/wiki/Advanced_Game_Settings "Advanced Game Settings") disables ability for all achievements to be awarded.[7][8]

The use of [mods](https://ficsit.app/mods?p=0) or save editors will not disable the ability for achievements to be awarded.[9][10]

### Offline play

Any achievements earned while playing offline will NOT be awarded after you go online. Achievements unlocked by triggering a certain action can be triggered again, but achievements related to progression are lost unless the last online save is loaded. This applies to both Steam and Epic Games.[11]

## Trivia

  * On August 29, 2024, the Steam client was updated to list the achievements expected to release with version 1.0.[13]
    * This change did not happen on the Epic Games Store.
    * Due to in-game triggers not being implemented, achievements couldn't be awarded to players before [Patch 1.0](/wiki/Patch_1.0 "Patch 1.0")
  * During the Closed Alpha phase, [Coffee Stain Studios](/wiki/Coffee_Stain_Studios "Coffee Stain Studios") had stated that achievements will most likely be added after the Alpha period to prevent exploitation due to bugs or imbalances.[14] Later, in 2020, they stated that achievements won't come any time soon until the game is finalized in design and balance.[15]
