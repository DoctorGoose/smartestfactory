## Lifeform

### Behavior

Stationary

The **Huge Pink Jellyfish** was an unofficial name given to a species of jellyfish-looking creatures. It was unknown whether it was Fauna or Flora. In a Q&A livestream it is referred to as "Big Jellyfish".[1] They use to appear in the [Red Jungle](/wiki/Red_Jungle "Red Jungle"), [Titan Forest](/wiki/Titan_Forest "Titan Forest") twice, and [Abyss Cliffs](/wiki/Abyss_Cliffs "Abyss Cliffs").

## Appearance

Huge Pink Jellyfishes had tentacle-like appendages and their body emited a pink light, their upper outer shell had a fine pattern and is purple in color. Those jellyfishes who were hanging from ceilings of caves contained several red petals or leaves extended outwards and several strings that stuck to the ceiling which supports them midair. 

## Behavior

All Huge Pink Jellyfishes appeared to be hovering in mid-air or from ceilings of caves. They were stationary and could not be killed or removed.
