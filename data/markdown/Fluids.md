# Fluids

There are fifteen different **fluids** of different purposes in _Satisfactory_. They are transported with [Pipelines](/wiki/Pipeline "Pipeline") and can be stored in [Fluid Buffers](/wiki/Fluid_Buffer "Fluid Buffer"). To the player, their amount is shown in cubic meters (m3) with up to 2 decimals, however, internally the game stores them in liters (1/1000 of a cubic meter). 

Unlike items, fluids cannot be carried in the [inventory](/wiki/Inventory "Inventory") unless packaged. 

The term "[fluid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fluid "wikipedia:Fluid")" collectively refers to liquids and gases.

## Gases

There are five gases currently used in the game. 

All gases lack the [head lift](/wiki/Head_lift "Head lift") requirements and thus do not require the use of [Pipeline Pumps](/wiki/Pipeline_Pump "Pipeline Pump").

## History

  * [Patch 1.0](/wiki/Patch_1.0 "Patch 1.0"): 
    * Introduced Dissolved Silica, Rocket Fuel, Ionized Fuel, Excited Photonic Matter and Dark Matter Residue
  * [Patch 0.4.0.11](/wiki/Patch_0.4.0.11 "Patch 0.4.0.11"): 
    * Default recipe for Nitric Acid: 
      * Changed Nitrogen Gas cost from 24 to 12
      * Changed Water cost from 6 to 3
      * Changed Nitric Acid product from 6 to 3
    * [Patch 0.4.0.8](/wiki/Patch_0.4.0.8 "Patch 0.4.0.8"): 
      * Alternate: Sloppy Alumina: 
        * Changed recipe craft time from 6 to 3 seconds
        * Changed Bauxite cost from 15 to 10
        * Changed Water cost from 15 to 10
        * Changed Alumina Solution product from 18 to 12
  * [Patch 0.4.0.0](/wiki/Patch_0.4.0.0 "Patch 0.4.0.0"): 
    * Introduced Nitrogen Gas Resource Wells and Nitrogen Gas, along with Nitric Acid
    * Introduced specific UI for gases and a separate system for them, meaning they lack a dependency on head lift.
    * Nerfed Sulfuric Acid recipe, now produces half as much from the same ingredients
    * Now Sulfuric Acid can be packaged
    * Introduced Turbo Blend Fuel alternate recipe
    * Fuel energy value increased from 600 MJ to 750 MJ
    * Water now extractable from resource wells
    * Water is now more light bluish in various buildings' UI 
      * Introduced Sloppy Alumina alternate recipe
      * Default recipe for Alumina Solution: 
        * Changed Bauxite cost from 7 to 12
        * Changed Water cost form 10 to 18
        * Changed Alumina Solution product from 8 to 12
        * Changed Silica product from 2 to 5
  * [Patch 0.3.3.4](/wiki/Patch_0.3.3.4 "Patch 0.3.3.4"): 
    * Unpackaging Heavy Oil Residue now gives back the right amount of Canisters
  * [Patch 0.3.3.2](/wiki/Patch_0.3.3.2 "Patch 0.3.3.2"): 
    * New icon for Turbofuel
    * New icon for Fuel
  * [Patch 0.3.1.0](/wiki/Patch_0.3.1.0 "Patch 0.3.1.0"): 
    * Liquid Fuel color was changed to orange due to popular demand
  * [Patch 0.3](/wiki/Patch_0.3 "Patch 0.3"): 
    * Introduced all liquids except Nitric Acid and Dissolved Silica
    * Crude Oil can no longer be brought by [Lizard Doggos](/wiki/Lizard_Doggo "Lizard Doggo")
    * Crude Oil can no longer be obtained by cutting down specific plants with the [Chainsaw](/wiki/Chainsaw "Chainsaw")
    * Crude Oil blobs occasionally found in foliage now result in [No item in foliage](/wiki/No_item_in_foliage "No item in foliage")
    * Loading a save created before Patch 0.3 would convert Turbofuel items to [Packaged Turbofuel](/wiki/Packaged_Turbofuel "Packaged Turbofuel")
    * Loading a save created before Patch 0.3 would convert Fuel items to [Packaged Fuel](/wiki/Packaged_Fuel "Packaged Fuel")
  * Unknown patch before [Patch 0.1](/wiki/Patch_0.1 "Patch 0.1"): 
    * Crude Oil no longer possible to be mined by hand
  * [Patch Closed Alpha 4](/wiki/Patch_Closed_Alpha_4 "Patch Closed Alpha 4"): 
    * Crude Oil mesh updated
