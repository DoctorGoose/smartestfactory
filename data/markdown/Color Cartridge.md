## Usage

**Color Cartridges** were used to apply patterns on [foundations](/wiki/Foundations "Foundations") in the [Customizer](/wiki/Customizer "Customizer"). 

### Fuel

Color Cartridges could also be used as a fuel for [vehicles](/wiki/Vehicles "Vehicles"). At 900 MJ, they had the 3rd highest energy from non-radioactive fuel items in the game after [](/wiki/Battery "Battery") [Batteries](/wiki/Battery "Battery") (6000 MJ) and [](/wiki/Packaged_Turbofuel "Packaged Turbofuel") [Packaged Turbofuel](/wiki/Packaged_Turbofuel "Packaged Turbofuel") (2000 MJ). 

They could not be used as a fuel for [Biomass Burner](/wiki/Biomass_Burner "Biomass Burner").
