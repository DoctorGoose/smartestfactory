## Construction

The Personnel Elevator is comprised of Floor Stops that can be stacked one on top of another. You can build maximum of 25 floor stops for a single elevator _(seeTrivia below)_. 

Once you do the initial elevator placement, you will be able to [zoop](/wiki/Build_Gun#Zoop "Build Gun") vertically to desired your elevation and add as many Elevator Floor Stops as you wish to match with your factory design. 

You will be able to customize the names for each floor as well as the icons and colours for each button that you can press to access each floor so you can keep track of exactly where each stop leads. 

The Personnel Elevator has a maximum height of 196 meters, which is the maximum height a single elevator can reach. While you can't extend it horizontally, you can build multiple elevators next to each other to reach even greater heights. 

## Usage

The elevator can be called by interacting with a button on any of its stops. Inside, the target floor can be selected by pressing the appropriate button on its panel. The main elevator UI (which is used to change both the music playing and the floor names) can be accessed from under the floor selection panel inside the elevator cabin. 

Once the elevator starts moving towards a floor, it will not stop (even if the stop is unselected). 

A Personnel Elevator only draws 20 MW of power while moving and 0.1 MW otherwise. If a [power trip](/wiki/Power_trip "Power trip") occurs while it is in motion, it will reverse back into the stop it came from. 

Each floor stop has a [Power Line](/wiki/Power_Line "Power Line") connection. The elevator acts as a power line between the floor stops: only one of the floor stops needs to be connected to power for the elevator to function, and connections at multiple floor stops will result in all of them being part of the same [Power Grid](/wiki/Power_Grid "Power Grid").

The interface of a Personnel Elevator




### Music

Each Personnel Elevator features a built-in music player, which emanates from the elevator and can be heard both inside and outside. The following tracks can be selected: 

  * Fiscanova
  * Fantastic Lift
  * Hiss Hiss
  * PElevating Music
  * OdeToElevator
  * PElevation of Conciousness
  * Personnel Elevator
  * Plifts And Chill
  * Tactical Elevator Action



Additionally, the tracks can be shuffled or muted.

## Trivia

  * While a Personnel Elevator is designed to only go to 25 floor stops / 196 Meters, you _can technically_ exceed that by using [Hologram Lock](/wiki/Build_Gun#Hologram_lock_and_nudge_mode "Build Gun").[3]
  * If a Personnel Elevator stop name has brackets in it (For example: [PERSONAL] Elevator), the text in brackets will be highlighted with a black color without the brackets shown.
