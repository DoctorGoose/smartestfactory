## Closed Beta

Following [Patch 0.8.3.3](/wiki/Patch_0.8.3.3 "Patch 0.8.3.3"), the development of Satisfactory continued in a Closed Beta, to be followed by version 1.0.[1.0 1][Beta 1] There will be no more patches in Early Access. The Experimental branch will not be used for 1.0 content. 

The Closed Beta opened for a limited number of players,[Beta 2] later revealed to be around 1,000.[Beta 3]

Access could be requested via a form on the [official website](https://www.satisfactorygame.com/#closedbeta). 

### Duration

The Closed Beta officially started on April 10, 2024, with the release of "closed beta keys".[Beta 4] It lasted "a couple of weeks"[Beta 3] and ended by May 2024. 

### Unreal Engine 5.3

The Closed Beta and version 1.0 will upgrade to [Unreal Engine 5.3](https://www.unrealengine.com/en-US/blog/unreal-engine-5-3-is-now-available).[Beta 5]
