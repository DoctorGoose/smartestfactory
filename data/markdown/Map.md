## Map

[ ](/wiki/File:Map_Icon.png "Map Icon.png")

### Unlocked by

[Tier 1](/wiki/Tier_1 "Tier 1") – Field Research

The **Map** , once unlocked in [Tier 1](/wiki/Tier_1 "Tier 1"), allows [pioneers](/wiki/Pioneer "Pioneer") to view areas of the [world](/wiki/World "World") that they either have explored or scanned using [Radar Towers](/wiki/Radar_Tower "Radar Tower"). In a [multiplayer](/wiki/Multiplayer "Multiplayer") game, the map is shared amongst all players. 

It can be accessed with [`M`](/wiki/Controls "Controls") and zoomed from 0.5x to 8x with the slider or mouse wheel. It can be dragged around with [](/wiki/File:Keyboard_White_Mouse_Right.png "Right"). 

The pioneer or Radar Towers have to be positioned above the terrain to reveal it (for example, when traveling through a [cave](/wiki/Cave "Cave"), the terrain above the cave is not revealed).

## Unlocking

The in-game map can be unlocked at [Tier 1](/wiki/Tier_1 "Tier 1") \- Field Research. Before it is unlocked, the shortcut in the bottom right of the screen will appear as a blank window with no key displayed.

## Usage

### Menu

The in-game map has a menu that is accessed in the upper left corner.[1]

You can choose to show or hide individual Markers on the [Compass](/wiki/Compass "Compass") and/or on the in-game map. You also can choose to show or hide all the Stamps on the in-game map. 

There is also a way to search for a specific Marker on the in-game map. 

### Coordinates

In the upper right corner of the in-game map you will see coordinates displaying both X and Y horizontal values showing the map coordinate relative to the position of the mouse cursor on the Map.[2]

You can also enter a specific coordinate (in X value, Y value) and there will be a "ping" on the Map showing the coordinate entered. 

Vertical (elevation) Z values can't be entered on the in-game map. 

### Icons

The following icons show up on the in-game map. Hovering over some reveals additional information: 

  * Markers
  * Stamps
  * Pioneers
  * [Crates](/wiki/Crate "Crate") \- Death Crates and Dismantle Crates
  * [Vehicles](/wiki/Vehicles "Vehicles") (excluding [Freight Cars](/wiki/Freight_Car "Freight Car"))
  * [The HUB](/wiki/The_HUB "The HUB") (active [milestone](/wiki/Milestones "Milestones"))
  * [Space Elevator](/wiki/Space_Elevator "Space Elevator") (Project Assembly phase and completion progress)
  * [MAM](/wiki/MAM "MAM") (current research)
  * [Radar Towers](/wiki/Radar_Tower "Radar Tower")
  * [Resource Nodes](/wiki/Resource_Node "Resource Node") and [Resource Wells](/wiki/Resource_Well "Resource Well")' central nodes scanned within the past 25 seconds
  * [Truck Stations](/wiki/Truck_Station "Truck Station") (contents and loading mode)
  * [Train Stations](/wiki/Train_Station "Train Station") (power consumption)
  * [Drone Ports](/wiki/Drone_Port "Drone Port") (incoming and outcoming contents and [Drone](/wiki/Drone "Drone") status)
  * [Portals](/wiki/Portal "Portal")



Powered [Radar Towers](/wiki/Radar_Tower "Radar Tower") additionally display some additional information in their range. [Resource Nodes](/wiki/Resource_Node "Resource Node") and [Resource Wells](/wiki/Resource_Well "Resource Well")' central nodes display permanently, not just 25 seconds after being scanned. The amount of [Power Slugs](/wiki/Power_Slug "Power Slug"), [Somersloops](/wiki/Somersloop "Somersloop"), [Mercer Spheres](/wiki/Mercer_Sphere "Mercer Sphere") and [Crash Sites](/wiki/Crash_Site "Crash Site") is listed each and species of flora and fauna are shown, however, their location is not. 

The following does _not_ show up on the in-game map: 

  * Other player built structures not listed above
  * Exact location of Power Slugs, Somersloops, Mercer Spheres and Crash Sites
  * Resource Deposits
  * Creatures
  * [Poison Gas](/wiki/Poison_Gas "Poison Gas")
  * [Cracked boulders](/wiki/Cracked_boulder "Cracked boulder")



### Markers and Stamps

Markers and Stamps are custom in-game map icons which can be placed freely by players.[3]

  * Markers have a customizable name, size, icon (such as home, vehicle, water, warning, hazard, etc.), color, and Compass view distance (respectively "invisible", 500 m, 1 km, 2.5 km, and "infinite" radius).
  * Stamps don't have any customization options, but can be placed in the world without opening the in-game map using [`Alt`](/wiki/Controls "Controls") \+ [](/wiki/File:Keyboard_White_Mouse_Right.png "Right").[4] Previously placed Stamps can be seen by pressing [`Alt`](/wiki/Controls "Controls") key while exploring the world.
  * Markers and Stamps can be removed by clicking on them, then clicking **Remove Marker** in pop-up customization dialog, and then clicking **Apply**. This can be done while exploring, or by accessed the Map with [`M`](/wiki/Controls "Controls").


  * [](/wiki/File:Map_Marker_customization.png "Marker customization dialog")

Marker customization dialog




#### Highlighted

Both Markers and Stamps can be highlighted using the UI when hovering over a Marker or Stamp on the in-game map.[5]

After highlighting a Marker or Stamp _that location_ will show a **Laser Beacon** in the [World](/wiki/World "World") showing the location of the highlighted Marker or Stamp.[6] This makes it easy to find the location and aids in exploration. 

  * [](/wiki/File:Highlight_Marker_Laser.png "Update 6 - Example use of Highlight Marker \(or Stamp\) which shows a Laser Beacon in the World.")

Update 6 - Example use of Highlight Marker (or Stamp) which shows a Laser Beacon in the World.




#### Available

In the lower right corner you will see the number of Markers and Stamps used versus the maximum allowed on the in-game map.[7] The maximum allowed Markers and Stamps is subject to change in a future game update or patch. 

### Player structures

The in-game map does not have the ability to display factory plans or any other player-placed buildings, only basic details are displayed. To be able to view the factory plan on the map, upload the [save file](/wiki/Save_file "Save file") to an [online map](/wiki/Online_tools "Online tools") instead.
