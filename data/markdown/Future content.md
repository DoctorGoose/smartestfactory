## Consoles

At end of Satisfactory 1.0 Launch Trailer[Console 1] it was revealed that Satisfactory would be coming to [Consoles](/wiki/Consoles "Consoles") (PlayStation and Xbox) at some point in the future. 

[Coffee Stain Studios](/wiki/Coffee_Stain_Studios "Coffee Stain Studios"), in cooperation with [FishLabs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fishlabs), who will be porting the game, will be releasing Satisfactory on consoles for PlayStation and Xbox at some point in the future.[Console 2]

### Release date

  * The release date is currently slated for **November 4, 2025.**[Console 3][Console 4][Console 5]
  * Satisfactory will be available on Xbox Series X/S and PlayStation 5 (PS5), and will feature Update 1.1 content and features.[Console 6]



### PC/Console cross-play

It has been confirmed there will **not** be cross play between consoles and PC. However there will be cross play between consoles.[Console 7]

  * Dedicated servers will not be available to console players.[Console 8]
  * Saves will not be transferrable from PC to console. However it has been bugging some of the developers so in the future it might happen.[Console 9]



## Version 1.2

The release of Version 1.2 for the game, sometime next year, has been confirmed.[1.2 1]

### Fluid update

  * Both [Oil Extractor](/wiki/Oil_Extractor "Oil Extractor") and [Water Extractor](/wiki/Water_Extractor "Water Extractor") can now extract Oil and Water from shallow water areas.[1.2 2]
  * Fluid Truck Station for fluid delivery and pickup with pipeline inputs / outputs.[1.2 3]
  * Tanker Truck for fluids.[1.2 3]



## DLC

### Release date

No DLC will be released prior to release of Version 1.1 and game release to Consoles in 2025.[DLC 1]

### Trains

A reference to DLC for a second "choo" for the Trains was made in February 2022.[DLC 2][DLC 3]

### Tick Armor

A reference to DLC for hard hats for hats on [creatures](/wiki/Category:Fauna "Category:Fauna") such as the [Space Giraffe-Tick-Penguin-Whale Thing](/wiki/Space_Giraffe-Tick-Penguin-Whale_Thing "Space Giraffe-Tick-Penguin-Whale Thing") has been referenced as being $2.50.[DLC 4]

### Deluxe Edition

Coffee Stain Studios have been "discussing the possibility" of deluxe editions of the game on release.[DLC 5]

### Pink Coffee Cup Pack

Snutt joked in July 2nd, 2024's livestream about the anticipation of a Pink Coffee Cup DLC.[DLC 6]

## Unspecified

  * Coffee Stain are looking into revamping the tutorial and very beginning of the game to provide a much easier entry into the game, and to bring in more people to the game and not scare them. [Unspecified 1] They specifically asked for the community to provide them with ideas and their own thoughts on how this could be accomplished in the future.[Unspecified 1]
  * Coffee Stain Studios is considering a option wherein, upon joining a [Multiplayer](/wiki/Multiplayer "Multiplayer") session or [Dedicated Server](/wiki/Dedicated_Server "Dedicated Server"), you will be able to pick which character you wish to spawn as _(i.e. "possess")_. [Unspecified 2]
  * There is content that has been "semi-finished" but not included in the 1.0 release to avoid the risk of additional issues occurring.[Unspecified 3]
