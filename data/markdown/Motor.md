### AWESOME Sink

**[](/wiki/Motor "Motor")Motor** can be sunk into the [](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") [AWESOME Sink](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") to yield **1,520 points**.

  


## Tips

  * In the early game, there are Motors scattered around some [Crash Sites](/wiki/Crash_Site "Crash Site") which can be fed into the [AWESOME Sink](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") for some quick [Coupons](/wiki/FICSIT_Coupon "FICSIT Coupon").
  * It may be convenient to set up an entire vertically integrated factory making Motors from base elements. Using default recipes, one Motor takes 31.5 [Iron Ore](/wiki/Iron_Ore "Iron Ore"), 8 [Copper Ore](/wiki/Copper_Ore "Copper Ore") and 9 [Coal](/wiki/Coal "Coal"). Creation from scratch involves two [Smelter](/wiki/Smelter "Smelter"), one [Foundry](/wiki/Foundry "Foundry"), four [Constructor](/wiki/Constructor "Constructor") and three [Assembler](/wiki/Assembler "Assembler") recipes.
  * Using the Iron Pipe and Iron Wire alternate recipes (optionally the Steel Rotor alt as well for a more streamlined production line), it is possible to completely automate Motor production using only [Iron](/wiki/Iron "Iron") nodes, although the amount of Iron needed is somewhat large due to the poor (4:1) conversion ratio of [Iron Ingots](/wiki/Iron_Ingot "Iron Ingot") to [Steel Pipes](/wiki/Steel_Pipe "Steel Pipe").
