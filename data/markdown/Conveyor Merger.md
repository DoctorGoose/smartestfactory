The **Conveyor Merger** is a logistics building that merges up to three input conveyors in sequence and outputs all resources onto the output conveyor.

## Construction

Conveyor Mergers can be either built separately, stacked on top of another Merger or [Conveyor Splitter](/wiki/Conveyor_Splitter "Conveyor Splitter"), or built directly onto a [Conveyor Belt](/wiki/Conveyor_Belt "Conveyor Belt"). Building directly onto a Belt makes the placement not follow [foundation](/wiki/Foundation "Foundation") grid alignments. 

Two Mergers (or Splitters) fit on one foundation without clipping through each other. 

### Upgrading and changing

  * Merger can be changed to a [Splitter](/wiki/Splitter "Splitter") or [Smart Splitter](/wiki/Smart_Splitter "Smart Splitter") or [Programmable Splitter](/wiki/Programmable_Splitter "Programmable Splitter"), as long as **only the main output is connected** , by holding **Snap To Guidelines/Snap Mode** ( [`Ctrl`](/wiki/Controls "Controls") by default) while aiming any Splitter variant hologram at any other already built Merger.



## Usage

A Conveyor Merger can be easily distinguished from a Conveyor Splitter (all types), as it has **three inputs** that are labelled ≡ and are orange-colored at the top _(unless[customized to use a different color](/wiki/Customizer "Customizer"))_, while it has a **single output** that is labelled with a 〕 arrow and is silver-colored at the top. 

The Conveyor Merger attempts to continuously take resources from the connected 1-3 conveyors and output them onto the output conveyor. If any input is not connected or empty, it takes resources from other connected conveyors instead. The Mk. of a conveyor does not affect the priority, for example, when merging a Mk.4 and Mk.2 conveyor onto a Mk.4, the input Mk.4 will have pauses to allow resources from the Mk.2 belt to be merged. 

The Merger has an internal inventory of one item. 

## Types

### Basic

The standard merger plainly combines items from its inputs in sequence. If any input is empty, it will combine the items from its other inputs evenly. 

### Priority

_Main article:_[Priority Merger](/wiki/Priority_Merger "Priority Merger")

A variant of the standard merger with a configurable priority for each of its three inputs. 

### Vertical

_Main article:_[Conveyor Lifts](/wiki/Conveyor_Lifts "Conveyor Lifts")

[Version 1.1](/wiki/Patch_1.1.0.0 "Patch 1.1.0.0") added ability to place mergers _(which become**Vertical Mergers**)_ on [Conveyor Lifts](/wiki/Conveyor_Lifts "Conveyor Lifts") allowing more modularity to your vertical builds.

  * [Patch 1.1.0.2](/wiki/Patch_1.1.0.2 "Patch 1.1.0.2")
    * Fixed crash related to [Conveyor Splitter](/wiki/Conveyor_Splitter "Conveyor Splitter") and Conveyor Mergers in some scenarios
    * Fixed visual artifacts on the Input/Output after dismantling a [Conveyor Splitter](/wiki/Conveyor_Splitter "Conveyor Splitter")/Conveyor Merger attached to a [Conveyor Lift](/wiki/Conveyor_Lifts "Conveyor Lifts") that is attached to a [Floor hole](/wiki/Conveyor_Lift_Floor_Hole "Conveyor Lift Floor Hole")

    * The [Splitter](/wiki/Splitter "Splitter") and Mergers capabilities have now improved, and they can now be attached to [Conveyor Lifts](/wiki/Conveyor_Lift "Conveyor Lift"), allowing more modularity to your vertical builds 
      * Splitters or Mergers will attach to both the Input or Output of a Conveyor Lift, as well to any point in-between, for extra flexibility in your builds
  * [Patch 1.0.0.5](/wiki/Patch_1.0.0.5 "Patch 1.0.0.5"): Fixed a bug where it was possible to merge a [Pipeline](/wiki/Pipeline "Pipeline") or a [Conveyor Belt](/wiki/Conveyor_Belt "Conveyor Belt") into itself by using a [Pipeline Junction](/wiki/Pipeline_Junction "Pipeline Junction") or [Splitter](/wiki/Splitter "Splitter")/Merger, which would cause duplications
  * [Patch 0.8.2.0](/wiki/Patch_0.8.2.0 "Patch 0.8.2.0")
    * Can now be upgraded to [Smart](/wiki/Smart_Splitter "Smart Splitter") or [Programmable](/wiki/Programmable_Splitter "Programmable Splitter") versions
    * Can also be upgraded to each other ([Splitter](/wiki/Splitter "Splitter") to Merger and vice-versa) as long as only the main input and output is connected
  * [Patch 0.8.0.1](/wiki/Patch_0.8.0.1 "Patch 0.8.0.1"): Fixed a crash when trying to upgrade a splitter/merger
  * [Patch 0.7.0.2](/wiki/Patch_0.7.0.2 "Patch 0.7.0.2"): Rewrote [Splitter](/wiki/Conveyor_Splitter "Conveyor Splitter")/Conveyor Merger code to make it better

  * [Patch 0.5.0.6](/wiki/Patch_0.5.0.6 "Patch 0.5.0.6"): Changed conveyor attachments (Conveyor Merger, [Conveyor Splitter](/wiki/Conveyor_Splitter "Conveyor Splitter"), [Smart Splitter](/wiki/Smart_Splitter "Smart Splitter"), and [Programmable Splitter](/wiki/Programmable_Splitter "Programmable Splitter")) to have soft clearance
  * [Patch 0.2.1.10](/wiki/Patch_0.2.1.10 "Patch 0.2.1.10"): Now has an internal inventory of 9 items
  * [Patch 0.1.5](/wiki/Patch_0.1.5 "Patch 0.1.5"): New design
