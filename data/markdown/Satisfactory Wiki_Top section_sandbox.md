About Factorio

**[Factorio](/wiki/Satisfactory "Satisfactory")** is a game in which you build and maintain factories. You will be mining resources, researching technologies, building infrastructure, automating production and fighting enemies. 

In the beginning you will find yourself chopping trees, mining ores and crafting mechanical arms and transport belts by hand, but in short time you can become an industrial powerhouse, with huge solar fields, oil refining and cracking, manufacture and deployment of construction and logistic robots, all for your resource needs. However this heavy exploitation of the planet's resources does not sit nicely with the locals, so you will have to be prepared to defend yourself and your machine empire. 

Join forces with other players in cooperative [Multiplayer](/wiki/Multiplayer "Multiplayer"), create huge factories, collaborate and delegate tasks between you and your friends. **[... read more ...](/wiki/Satisfactory "Satisfactory")**

Features

  * A variety of item and fluid logistics including [Conveyor Belts](/wiki/Conveyor_Belts "Conveyor Belts"), [Lifts](/wiki/Conveyor_Lift "Conveyor Lift"), [Pipes](/wiki/Pipeline "Pipeline") and more; all for transporting resources, components, fluids or yourself around.
  * [Buildings](/wiki/Buildings "Buildings") for automated production of items. Hunt down [Power Slugs](/wiki/Power_Slug "Power Slug") to improve their [performance](/wiki/Clock_speed "Clock speed").
  * A vast alien [world](/wiki/World "World") to explore and exploit, featuring beautiful vistas and curious [creatures](/wiki/Fauna "Fauna"), including the community favorite [Lizard Doggo](/wiki/Lizard_Doggo "Lizard Doggo").
  * [Vehicles](/wiki/Vehicles "Vehicles"), including [trucks](/wiki/Truck "Truck") and [trains](/wiki/Electric_Locomotive "Electric Locomotive") for transporting yourself and large quantities of resources over great distances.
  * [Arachnophobia Mode](/wiki/Arachnophobia_Mode "Arachnophobia Mode"), to turn [scary spiders](/wiki/Stinger "Stinger") into equally terrifying cat abominations.
  * No base defense.
