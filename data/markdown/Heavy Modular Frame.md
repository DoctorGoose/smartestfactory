[ ](/wiki/File:Heavy_Modular_Frame.png "Heavy Modular Frame.png")

### Stack size

50

The **Heavy Modular Frame** is a mid-game crafting component commonly used for mid to late-game structures. It is the successor to the [](/wiki/Modular_Frame "Modular Frame") [Modular Frame](/wiki/Modular_Frame "Modular Frame").

### AWESOME Sink

**[](/wiki/Heavy_Modular_Frame "Heavy Modular Frame")Heavy Modular Frame** can be sunk into the [](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") [AWESOME Sink](/wiki/AWESOME_Sink "AWESOME Sink") to yield **10,800 points**.

## Tips

Heavy Encased Frame (HEF) [Alternate Recipe](/wiki/Alternate_Recipes "Alternate Recipes") lowers belt load from over 239/m [Screws](/wiki/Screw "Screw") to under 21/m [Concrete](/wiki/Concrete "Concrete") and pairs well with other Alternate Recipes: [Encased Industrial Pipe](/wiki/EIB "EIB") and [Solid Steel Ingot](/wiki/Steel_Ingot "Steel Ingot"). Both conserve [Coal](/wiki/Coal "Coal"), improving logistics when upgrading factories. Heavy Flexible Frame (HFF) Alternate Recipe increases belt load to 390/m Screws and has similar name to get mixed up when researching for HEF.
