The **Particle Accelerator** is a [building](/wiki/Building "Building") used to craft advanced late-game recipes. Unlike most other production buildings, its power consumption fluctuates and varies per selected recipe. The least power is consumed at the beginning of a crafting cycle and it increases to the maximum throughout the duration of the crafting cycle.

_Main article:_[Clock speed](/wiki/Clock_speed "Clock speed")

## Tips

  * Given the massive power consumption of the Particle Accelerator, it is highly advisable to build more of them and underclock each, if there is enough space and materials to do so. For example, building twice as many and underclocking all to 50% reduces total power consumption by 20%, as well as allowing them to run at different intervals.
  * Prior to update 5, the collision box of the building was a solid cuboid. This meant most other buildings could not be built through the empty spaces of it, except [Railways](/wiki/Railway "Railway") and similar. Update 5 added complex clearance (complex collision meshes) to many buildings, including this one, allowing other buildings to be built inside much of its empty space.
  * [Power Storages](/wiki/Power_Storage "Power Storage") can be used to mitigate the effects of the fluctuating power consumption. To calculate the number of Power Storages required for this, we must consider the maximum charge rate of Power Storages and the recipe being used in the Particle Accelerator. While Power Storages can discharge unlimited amounts of power, they can only charge at a maximum rate of 100MW. For that reason, we need a number of Power Storages that is sufficient to consume all the excess power during the periods in which the Particle Accelerator is using less than average power. The Power Storages and Particle Accelerator together then have a fixed combined power demand of the average power consumption of each recipe. 
    * You would need 2.5 Power Storages per Particle Accelerator configured with either the Plutonium Pellet or Instant Plutonium Cell recipe. Average power consumption of the Particle Accelerator would be 500MW with minimum power consumption of 250MW, leaving a maximum of 250MW for charging Power Storages.
    * You would need 5 Power Storages per Particle Accelerator configured with the Nuclear Pasta recipe. Average power consumption of the Particle Accelerator would be 1000MW with minimum power consumption of 500MW, leaving 500MW for charging Power Storages.



## Trivia

  * In [Patch 0.4.0.11](/wiki/Patch_0.4.0.11 "Patch 0.4.0.11"), the [Nitrogen Gas](/wiki/Nitrogen_Gas "Nitrogen Gas") usage of the Instant Plutonium Cell recipe was removed, which meant that there was no recipe which used the building's [Pipeline](/wiki/Pipeline "Pipeline") input until [Patch 1.0](/wiki/Patch_1.0 "Patch 1.0"), which added several recipes with pipeline inputs.
  * The building is named "Hadron Collider" internally, which was its in-game name until it was properly implemented in Update 4.

    * Changed 100 Electromagnetic Control Rod cost to 50 Cooling System
    * Changed Fused Modular Frame cost from 20 to 25
