The **Tractor** (aka **"The Sugarcube"**[1]) is a small, wheeled [vehicle](/wiki/Vehicle "Vehicle") which can be either driven by the [pioneer](/wiki/Pioneer "Pioneer") or have its movements automated. This multipurpose vehicle is the the first (off-road) vehicle the pioneer has access to, being unlocked in Tier 3.[2] It can interact with [Truck Stations](/wiki/Truck_Station "Truck Station") to load and unload cargo and is succeeded by the [Truck.](/wiki/Truck "Truck")

The Tractor will destroy any small vegetation, such as bushes and small trees, on collision. Other than pioneers, the vehicle's 25-slot inventory can be used to transport large amounts of resources. However, it is a relatively slow vehicle and handles rough terrain poorly when compared to other in-game vehicles (excluding the [factory cart](/wiki/Factory_Cart "Factory Cart")).

## Usage

_Main article:_[Vehicles](/wiki/Vehicles "Vehicles")

The Tractor's inventory slots can be accessed by interacting with a red box in the rear of the vehicle. Along with inventory interaction, fueling this unit is achieved by inputting up to a stack of fuel into the fuel slot. All fuels are accepted, ranging from [](/wiki/Leaves "Leaves") [Leaves](/wiki/Leaves "Leaves") to [](/wiki/Plutonium_Fuel_Rod "Plutonium Fuel Rod") [Plutonium Fuel Rods](/wiki/Plutonium_Fuel_Rod "Plutonium Fuel Rod"). Not all fuels have the same energy potential and those with more energy potential last longer, but don't otherwise affect anything with the vehicle. Additionally, the built-in [Craft Bench](/wiki/Craft_Bench "Craft Bench") can be accessed from the same menu, by using a tabber located at the top of the menu. 

Once the Tractor is fueled, the Pioneer can enter the vehicle and drive it. If the Tractor runs out of fuel while driving the Pioneer is prevented from accelerating and braking. Automated routes can be recorded using the self-driving menu [`Q`](/wiki/Controls "Controls"). 

Be careful near the edge of the map. If a tractor falls off it won't respawn, and the icon will remain on the map with no way to remove it.
