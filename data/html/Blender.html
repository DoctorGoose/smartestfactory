The Blender is a late-game building used for a wide variety of late-game recipes involving both fluids and solid resources.

The color of the fluid in the blending chamber changes depending on the recipe chosen. For example, Cooling System makes the fluid blue.

Overclocking

Main article: Clock speed

The Blender can be overclocked using Power Shards . Overclocking increases the input/output speed of the Blender at the cost of exponentially increased power demand.

Clock speed 25% 50% 75% 100% 150% 200% 250%
Consumption (MW) 12 30 51.27 75 128.19 187.5 251.83

Byproducts

The Blender stops working if either of its outputs backs up, similar to a Refinery . To keep the Blender running, All its products must be constantly removed from it.

Head lift

Main article: Head lift

The Blender has a recommended head lift of 10 meters, measured from the center of its Pipeline output.

Tips

  • When using the Diluted Fuel alternate recipe, a convenient ratio would be five Heavy Oil Residue Refineries to four Diluted Fuel Blenders. Each minute this will consume 150 Crude Oil and produce 400 Fuel and 100 Polymer Resin . The resulting Polymer Resin neatly satisfies one Residual Rubber Refinery and one Residual Plastic Refinery, resulting in 20 Rubber and 20 Plastic each minute. The total water utilized by this system will require four Water Extractors with one underclocked by 83.3% to output 100/m³.
