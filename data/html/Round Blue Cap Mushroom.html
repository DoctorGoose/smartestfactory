Round Blue Cap Mushroom

Loot

12-15× Mycelia

Round Blue Cap Mushrooms are a type of bioluminescent mushroom found in caves and above ground in certain biomes . They can be harvested using a Chainsaw , yielding 12-15 Mycelia from a big mushroom and around eight from a medium-sized one.

Appearance

Round Blue Cap Mushrooms are large tall blue bioluminescent mushrooms that can grow to about six to eight meters. During the day they are very easily recognizable by their light blue color, and at night by their bluish glow.
