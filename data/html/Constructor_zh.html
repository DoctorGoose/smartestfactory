Constructor

Crafts 1 part into another part.
Can be automated by feeding component parts in via a Conveyor Belt connected to the input port. The resulting parts can be automatically extracted by connecting a Conveyor Belt to the output port.

超頻

Main article: Clock speed

The Constructor/zh can be overclocked using Power Shards . Overclocking increases the input/output speed of the Constructor/zh at the cost of exponentially increased power demand.

Clock speed 25% 50% 75% 100% 150% 200% 250%
Consumption (MW) 0.64 1.6 2.73 4 6.84 10 13.43

畫廊

  • 幾個製造機通過傳送帶連接到 冶煉機 。
  • 一排製造機在生產 混凝土 。

趣聞

模型上的序列號用駭客語翻譯為「製造機」和「我蓋章」。
