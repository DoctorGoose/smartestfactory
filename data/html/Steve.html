Removed content
This article or section is about a feature that has been removed from the game.

Caterina as well as Steve have been officially retconned from Satisfactory's story and universe as of 16th of August 2024. [ 1 ]

Steve

Steve was to be an assistant to Caterina Parks , an employee at FICSIT Inc. and the creator of the 'Introduction' on the original website describing some duties to the pioneer . He has been described as ambitious and very stressed. [ 2 ]

Former Speculation

  • Possibly a primary point of contact for the pioneer during their time working for FICSIT Inc.
  • The male voice in the game file 0379.wav saying "Huh, must have been a dream." possibly belonged to him. The sound is currently unused in the game, as Steve is no longer part of Satisfactory's cast or lore. It should be noted that a female voice (possibly Caterina's) said the exact same thing in the file 0926.wav .
