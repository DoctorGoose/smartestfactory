A forest in the Grass Fields

The Satisfactory world features many different species of flora .

Most of it can be cut down using a Chainsaw , which will grant Wood and Leaves (or small amounts of other resources, see below). It is highly profitable to cut down trees using the Chainsaw, as it will always grant more resources than what is needed for one Solid Biofuel .

It is possible to collect leaves or wood by holding E and moving.

Foliage cannot be planted and will never regrow, so with enough dedication, all of it can be removed from the world.

Hitbox

Most trees or plants with visible wooden parts in their body have hitboxes, which can cause obstructions to the pioneer 's and vehicle 's movement and also encroach buildings. Pioneers sometimes can get stuck within a tree but can be unstuck by crouching or sliding, by using a Chainsaw, or by respawning from the Esc menu. Some vehicles have the ability to knock down trees as well. When constructing buildings, and when using the auto-snapping mode, by holding down the Ctrl during placement, buildings will be automatically moved upwards to avoid small trees, while large trees will disable the building placement.

Bushes, or plants without wooden parts, including flowers, do not have hitboxes, so they will not cause obstructions.

Gigantic trees

Many species of trees have a gigantic variant. They cannot be removed by any means.
