Map

Unlocked by

Tier 1 – Field Research

The Map , once unlocked in Tier 1 , allows pioneers to view areas of the world that they either have explored or scanned using Radar Towers . In a multiplayer game, the map is shared amongst all players.

It can be accessed with M and zoomed from 0.5x to 8x with the slider or mouse wheel. It can be dragged around with .

The pioneer or Radar Towers have to be positioned above the terrain to reveal it (for example, when traveling through a cave , the terrain above the cave is not revealed).

Unlocking

The in-game map can be unlocked at Tier 1 - Field Research. Before it is unlocked, the shortcut in the bottom right of the screen will appear as a blank window with no key displayed.

Usage

Menu

The in-game map has a menu that is accessed in the upper left corner. [ 1 ]

You can choose to show or hide individual Markers on the Compass and/or on the in-game map. You also can choose to show or hide all the Stamps on the in-game map.

There is also a way to search for a specific Marker on the in-game map.

Coordinates

In the upper right corner of the in-game map you will see coordinates displaying both X and Y horizontal values showing the map coordinate relative to the position of the mouse cursor on the Map. [ 2 ]

You can also enter a specific coordinate (in X value, Y value) and there will be a "ping" on the Map showing the coordinate entered.

Vertical (elevation) Z values can't be entered on the in-game map.

Icons

The following icons show up on the in-game map. Hovering over some reveals additional information:

  • Markers
  • Stamps
  • Pioneers
  • Crates - Death Crates and Dismantle Crates
  • Vehicles (excluding Freight Cars )
  • The HUB (active milestone )
  • Space Elevator (Project Assembly phase and completion progress)
  • MAM (current research)
  • Radar Towers
  • Resource Nodes and Resource Wells ' central nodes scanned within the past 25 seconds
  • Truck Stations (contents and loading mode)
  • Train Stations (power consumption)
  • Drone Ports (incoming and outcoming contents and Drone status)
  • Portals

Powered Radar Towers additionally display some additional information in their range. Resource Nodes and Resource Wells ' central nodes display permanently, not just 25 seconds after being scanned. The amount of Power Slugs , Somersloops , Mercer Spheres and Crash Sites is listed each and species of flora and fauna are shown, however, their location is not.

The following does not show up on the in-game map:

  • Other player built structures not listed above
  • Exact location of Power Slugs, Somersloops, Mercer Spheres and Crash Sites
  • Resource Deposits
  • Creatures
  • Poison Gas
  • Cracked boulders

Markers and Stamps

Markers and Stamps are custom in-game map icons which can be placed freely by players. [ 3 ]

  • Markers have a customizable name, size, icon (such as home, vehicle, water, warning, hazard, etc.), color, and Compass view distance (respectively "invisible", 500 m, 1 km, 2.5 km, and "infinite" radius).
  • Stamps don't have any customization options, but can be placed in the world without opening the in-game map using Alt + . [ 4 ] Previously placed Stamps can be seen by pressing Alt key while exploring the world.
  • Markers and Stamps can be removed by clicking on them, then clicking Remove Marker in pop-up customization dialog, and then clicking Apply . This can be done while exploring, or by accessed the Map with M .
  • Marker customization dialog

Highlighted

Both Markers and Stamps can be highlighted using the UI when hovering over a Marker or Stamp on the in-game map. [ 5 ]

After highlighting a Marker or Stamp that location will show a Laser Beacon in the World showing the location of the highlighted Marker or Stamp. [ 6 ] This makes it easy to find the location and aids in exploration.

  • Update 6 - Example use of Highlight Marker (or Stamp) which shows a Laser Beacon in the World.

Available

In the lower right corner you will see the number of Markers and Stamps used versus the maximum allowed on the in-game map. [ 7 ] The maximum allowed Markers and Stamps is subject to change in a future game update or patch.

Player structures

The in-game map does not have the ability to display factory plans or any other player-placed buildings, only basic details are displayed. To be able to view the factory plan on the map, upload the save file to an online map instead.
