Cracked boulder

Creates obstructions. Can be detonated.

Cracked boulders are large destructible stones that appear in the world solely to create obstructions. They can be blown up with Nobelisks or Explosive Rebars . Once destroyed, they will never re-appear.

Occurrence

Cracked boulders can appear:

  • On Resource Nodes , to block the construction of Miner buildings.
  • Covering up collectibles , such as Power Slugs .
  • Blocking a path, usually at the borders between biomes .
  • Blocking a cave entrance.

Method of Destruction

You can use the following to destroy them:

  • Nobelisk
  • Explosive Rebar

Trivia

  • There are 689 total cracked boulders in the world.
  • Before Nobelisks were introduced to the game, it was not possible to remove cracked boulders, rendering some Resource Nodes obstructed or areas inaccessible.
