  • Module:AwesomeSinkPoints

The Sinkusage template is used in the Usage section of parts under AWESOME Sink. It displays whether the part can be sunk into the AWESOME Sink and if it can, how many points it yields.

Usage

  • 1 – className of the target item – mandatory

Examples

{{ Sinkusage |Desc_Cable_C}}

Cable can be sunk into the AWESOME Sink to yield 24 points .

{{ Sinkusage |Desc_PlutoniumFuelRod_C}}

Plutonium Fuel Rod can be sunk into the AWESOME Sink to yield 153,184 points .
