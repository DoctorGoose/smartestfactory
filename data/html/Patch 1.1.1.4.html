The original post can be viewed on Satisfactory's Discord , Reddit , and Steam .

Intro

Hi Pioneers!

Hello again everyone, Here’s a patch with another attempt to fix the remaining Dedicated Server desync issues, worth mentioning that Surround sound not working properly since 1.1 Experimental should be addressed as of the previous patch, it was just accidentally not on the Patch Notes so It’s been added to the patch notes today

If there’s anything new that might have been introduced from this update, please let us know over at our QA Site https://questions.satisfactorygame.com/ We read your posts every day

See you all again soon <3

Anniversary

After the initial release on September 8, another post was published as patch notes on September 10, announcing the anniversary event, originally not listed in the patch notes from September 8. However, no actual patch was released and the build number remained as 433351.

The original post can be viewed on Discord , Reddit , and Steam .

Hi Pioneers

It's hard to believe it has been a year already since we officially launched 1.0 and left Early Access, the reception has been incredible and we're all extremely grateful that you all love Satisfactory

To celebrate our one-year anniversary we have decorated the HUB so you can enjoy it alongside us, the HUB will stay decorated for 2 weeks from the 10th of September until the 24th of September

And if you get tired of dancing, eating pizza, candy and cake and don't want to wait until the event ends you can always return the HUB back to default by going to Options > Gameplay > Disable Seasonal Events

Our Community Managers have prepared a video with some words from us all, which you can watch below:

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eZsxG3h6CqE

Thanks community, helps a lot <3
