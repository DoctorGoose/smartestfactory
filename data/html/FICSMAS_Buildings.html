Giant FICSMAS Tree

The Giant FICSMAS Tree comes with no decoration by default, where each upgrade research in the MAM added decorations onto it.

  1. The first upgrade adds Candy Canes.
  2. The second upgrade adds Red, Blue, and Copper ornaments, along with the FICSMAS Power Lights.
  3. The third upgrade adds Iron ornaments and colorful gift boxes to the tree.
  4. The final upgrade adds a decorative star at the tip of the tree.

Despite its description, multiple Giant FICSMAS Trees could be built.

FICSMAS Gift Tree

The FICSMAS Gift Tree produces 15 FICSMAS Gifts per minute. It has no GUI, consumes no power and outputs gifts as long as a conveyor belt is connected to its output and the date is between December 1st and January 18th. When 10 or more Gift Trees are placed within a world, Giant FICSMAS Gifts stop falling from the sky.

Being a production building, it has a hard clearance , and its clearance hitbox is taller than visuals suggest (17 m).
