Trivia

Some players report a Mandela Effect regarding the "Priority Splitter," believing it was introduced alongside the Priority Merger in Update 1.1 , and that it functioned similarly with three levels of priority for output ports. However, while the Priority Merger was added in that update, no such "Priority Splitter" has ever existed in the game.
