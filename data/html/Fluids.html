There are fifteen different fluids of different purposes in Satisfactory . They are transported with Pipelines and can be stored in Fluid Buffers . To the player, their amount is shown in cubic meters (m 3 ) with up to 2 decimals, however, internally the game stores them in liters (1/1000 of a cubic meter).

Unlike items, fluids cannot be carried in the inventory unless packaged.

The term " fluid " collectively refers to liquids and gases.

Gases

There are five gases currently used in the game.

All gases lack the head lift requirements and thus do not require the use of Pipeline Pumps .

Fluid Color Unlocked at Source Source building Packaged form Usage
Nitrogen Gas #595959 Tier 8 Nitrogen Gas Resource Well Resource Well Extractor Packaged Nitrogen Gas Advanced Aluminum parts
Rocket Fuel #BD251A Tier 8 ( MAM Sulfur Research ) Turbofuel + Nitric Acid Blender Packaged Rocket Fuel Fuel Generator , Vehicle fuel and Jetpack
Ionized Fuel #D55F1A Tier 9 ( MAM Sulfur Research ) Rocket Fuel + Power Shard Refinery Packaged Ionized Fuel Fuel Generator , Vehicle fuel and Jetpack
Excited Photonic Matter #76F5E8 Tier 9 Produced at no cost Converter Cannot be packaged Quantum Encoder
Dark Matter Residue #FDAFF9 Tier 9 All Quantum Encoder recipes, or Reanimated SAM Quantum Encoder or Converter Cannot be packaged Dark Matter Crystal and Ficsonium

Note: The use of Packaged Gases is recommended for long distance transport and increased throughput. This is especially noteworthy for train transport. Packaged Nitrogen Gas provides 4× compression while Packaged Rocket Fuel and Packaged Ionized Fuel provide 2× compression.

Excited Photonic Matter and Dark Matter Residue are technically not gases, but behave as such in the game. [ 1 ] [ 2 ]
