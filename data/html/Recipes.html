Recipes are conversions from a set of resources (ingredients) to another set of resources (products). There are three types of recipes:

  • Crafting recipes – Used to craft items and fluids in factory buildings, the Craft Bench , or Equipment Workshop . They are non-reversible; once a resource is crafted, it cannot be deconstructed back. However, packaging and unpackaging fluids in the Packager is reversible via mirrored recipes.
  • Building recipes – Used to construct buildings with the Build Gun . Buildings can be dismantled at no cost, returning all resources used to construct them, including their contents (if any).
  • Customizer recipes – Used to convert customizable buildings to another variant using the Customizer . Similarly to building recipes, all costs are refundable.

New recipes are unlocked from Milestones , Research , the AWESOME Shop and AWESOME Sink . Alternate recipes are unlocked by scanning lost Hard Drives found in Crash Sites . Some recipes are only available during seasonal events, such as FICSMAS .
