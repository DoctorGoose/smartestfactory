This is a generic infobox. It is intended for one-off situations where other, dedicated infobox templates wouldn't fit. It has a name, image, description, and two groups, each with five values. It is not intended to be used to create other infobox templates.

Usage 

{{infobox
| name = 
| image = 
| description = 
| section1 = 
| info1 = 
| info1value = 
| info2 = 
| info2value = 
| info3 = 
| info3value = 
| info4 = 
| info4value = 
| info5 = 
| info5value = 
| section2 = 
| info6 = 
| info6value = 
| info7 = 
| info7value = 
| info7 =
