No item in foliage

No item in foliage appears when aiming at foliage or rocks that yield no item. Trying to pick it up makes it simply disappear.

  • 1 Occurrence
  • 2 References
  • 3 Trivia

Occurrence

  • Crude Oil blobs in the Southern Forest
    • Used to yield Crude Oil barrels while it was an item prior to Patch 0.3 [ 1 ] alongside Crude Oil bearing plants
  • Some medium-sized rocks in the Dune Desert
    • As of Patch 0.4.0.0
  • Various rocks in caves
    • As of Patch 0.5.0.0 and more as of Patch 0.6.0.0

References

  1. ↑ Reddit - /r/SatisfactoryGame/ - What is that? "No item in foliage"

Trivia

  • The reflection in the shown Crude Oil blob is HDRI_Epic_Courtyard_Daylight, a default Unreal Engine texture cube used in reflective objects most of the time, unless lumen is on
    • The same texture is used in the Pioneer 's helmet reflection
