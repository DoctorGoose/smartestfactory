Satisfactory

Satisfactory is a first-person factory construction and automation game with single player and co-op multiplayer , made by Coffee Stain Studios in Unreal Engine 5 . [ 4 ] You play as an employee of FICSIT Inc. and must contribute towards Project Assembly .

As a pioneer , you are tasked with exploring the planet for resources, in order to construct machines , which will build necessary parts. As the game progresses, more complex machines and parts become available.

The game was initially released in Early Access exclusively on the Epic Games Store on March 19, 2019 at 5pm GMT. It later released on Steam on June 8, 2020 at 5pm GMT, with cross-platform multiplayer. Version 1.0 was launched on September 10, 2024 at 5pm GMT on the Epic Games Store and Steam, with a console release announced for a later but as of now undetermined date.

More information can be found at the official site or on the Dev Tracker .

Official description

Satisfactory is a first-person open-world factory building game with a dash of exploration and combat. Pioneering for FICSIT Incorporated means charting and exploiting an alien planet, battling alien lifeforms, creating multi-story factories, entering conveyor belt heaven, automating vehicles , and researching new technologies .

At FICSIT inc. you will take part in the Save the Day program, providing short-term solutions for long-term problems, possibly with the help of your friends.

Monetization

The game is a one-time purchase, and will not feature micro-transactions or loot boxes. There are plans to support the game post release, possibly with updates and/or DLC. It is unknown if these will be free or paid. [ 5 ]

On 5 August 2024 , before the release of 1.0 , the price was increased from $29.99 to $39.99. [ 3 ] The reason given is inflation and development time spent since the original release. [ 6 ]

Trivia

  • While in pre-Alpha, between 16-18 people worked on Satisfactory's development. [ 7 ]
  • Two out of three releases were made on time in 2020. [ 8 ]
  • Staff at Coffee Stain were surprised to learn that around the time of the Early Access launch, Satisfactory had more concurrent views than DOTA 2 (a major e-sports title). [ 9 ]
  • Satisfactory's name was hinted at in a 2017 tweet, [ 10 ] and later in a 2018 Q&A video. [ 11 ]
  • Satisfactory's pre-production started around 2016/2017. [ 12 ]
    • The game's development began with Unreal Engine 4 and continued to use it until Update 8, released in 2023, when the game upgraded to Unreal Engine 5. [ 4 ]
  • By December 2021, Satisfactory had sold 3 million copies. [ 13 ] As of December 2023, Satisfactory had sold 5.5 million copies. [ 14 ]
