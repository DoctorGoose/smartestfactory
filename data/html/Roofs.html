Roofs are a set of buildings which can be used to cover open areas of a factory. They can be painted , like other buildings. Roofs snap to other Walls . They are useful for decorating structures.

Types

All roofs share the following common properties:

  • Width: 8 m
  • Length: 8 m
  • Height: 0.5-4 m (depends on angle)
  • Build cost:
    • 2× Customizer Material
    • 3× Concrete

Construction

  • Roofs can be built one at a time or 10 at once using the Zoop construction mode.

Tips

  • Flat Roofs, which have a height of 0.51m, get placed in the center of a snapping point, meaning it will be 25.5cm above a Foundation . By using a combination of Pillars , Foundations and Frame Floors you can create foundations at any 1cm interval
