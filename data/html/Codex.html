The Codex window functions as the in-game encyclopedia. Not all the information is displayed from the beginning; instead, it only displays information for items that have been discovered by the player. It contains three tabs: Inbox, Tutorials, and Recipes.

Inbox

The inbox tab contains a log of messages from ADA and ??? , such as discoveries, milestone and research completion, etc. Clicking the 'play' button will playback the voice message from ADA and/or ???.

Tutorial

The tutorial tab contains a log of tutorial messages from ADA, such as from dismantling the drop pod , building the HUB , etc. Clicking the 'play' button will playback the voice message from ADA.

Recipes

The recipes tab can be used to view crafting recipes of all currently craftable/buildable items. Only items that can be currently crafted/built are shown (e.g. the recipe for Computers is only shown after unlocking the Tier 5 milestone: Industrial Manufacturing).

It has three sections, Parts, Equipment, and Buildings, showing recipes from the Craft Bench , Equipment Workshop and Build Gun respectively, except for Vehicles , which are shown in the Parts section.

All recipes can now be expanded to show the per-minute stats of all ingredients and products. You can also simulate overclocking in there now and see how that affects the recipes requirements and output.
