The anniversary is an annual seasonal event that takes place between September 10 and September 24 each year to celebrate Satisfactory 's 1.0 release on September 10, 2024. The first anniversary event was held in September 2025. [ 1 ]

The anniversary party in the HUB .

Prior to 1.0, the anniversary could be considered to be March 19, the release of Satisfactory Early Access , in 2019. However, no anniversary celebration events were held anytime during the entirety of Early Access.

The event festoons the HUB with anniversary decorations, to include a mirror ball, flashing tile dance floor, pizza boxes with various pizzas, music, and a festive cake adorned with a candle showing the year.

Participating

Upon loading of game save, the anniversary event in the HUB will be active unless it was disabled beforehand.

Disabling the event

The event can be disabled using the "Disable Seasonal Events" option. This option is only available on the title screen , under Options > Gameplay > Disable Seasonal Events .

In multiplayer , the host's configuration is used. If the host has seasonal events enabled, but the client does not, the event will be enabled in the session.

Alternatively, seasonal events can be disabled using the -DisableSeasonalEvents launch argument . On a dedicated server , this launch argument is the only way to disable seasonal events.

HUB decorations

Introduced with the Anniversary 2025 event, the HUB Living Quarters was festooned with a variety of decorations:

  • A mirror ball reflecting orange and blue alternating flashing lights.
  • Flashing multi-colored tile dance floor.
  • Flag garlands strung near the ceiling (one partially fallen) .
  • Around the Operation SCADA-HMI Terminal Desk area you see:
    • Drinks and Cups.
    • Music Speaker.
  • Bunk bed with a pizza.
  • Pantry birthday cake with a candle.
    • The cake was a Princesstarta (Prinsesstårta) , (aka Princess cake or Princess Torte ) which is a is a traditional Swedish layer cake.
    • The candle for the first event was in the form of a " 1 ".
  • Pantry pizza's with different toppings.
  • Toilet balloons, Lizard Doggo seat cover, and a "Its is Anniversary" garland above the mirror.
  • Space Giraffe-Tick-Penguin-Whale Thing piñata.
  • Balloons in the shape of a circle, Space Giraffe-Tick-Penguin-Whale Thing, and Somersloop .
  • Two cardboard cutouts of Pioneers (which you can standby and take photos with using Photo Mode decoupled camera )
  • Fog machine.

Piñata

There was a Space Giraffe-Tick-Penguin-Whale Thing piñata that could be destroyed, dropping snacks and confetti onto the ground. Upon destruction, a new piñata would drop down from the Conveyor Lift Floor Hole in the ceiling.

  • There are 4 types of snacks that are randomly dropped:
    • FiskIt (Bag) containing fish candy which are based upon the real world Swedish Fish .
    • FIX (Bag) containing unknown candy type (presumed chocolate) .
    • FIX (Candy) which is a pink oval treat.
    • Red Bar which is a chocolate bar.
  • There are lots of confetti that drops, some of which have recognizable shapes as follows:
    • Pink/Blue Pioneer (that might be mistaking for cats) that is speculated to relate to both types of game players.
    • Red Fish that relates to the real world Swedish Fish.
    • Blue Water Bottle (unknown meaning) .
