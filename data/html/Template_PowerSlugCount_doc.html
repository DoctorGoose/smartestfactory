This template creates a summary table of Power Slugs and their respective number of Power Shards .

Example: {{PowerSlugCount|blue = 5|yellow = 3|purple = 2}}

Slug Color Blue Yellow Purple Total
Slug Number 5 3 2 10
Power Shards 3 4 5 12

There are currently 10 Power Slugs known in-game; 5 blue, 3 yellow, and 2 purple. 1 slug of each type is consumed as part of research in the MAM . In addition, 1 Power Shard is consumed to unlock the overclocking function. The upper limit of obtainable Power Shards is infinite, as they can be synthesized in a Quantum Encoder and Lizard Doggos can generate an infinite amount of slugs over time. The upper limit of non-renewable, usable Power Shards is 12.
