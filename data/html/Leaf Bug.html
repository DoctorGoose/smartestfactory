Leaf Bug

Lifeform

Behavior

Passive

Leaf Bugs (unofficial name) are tiny flightless insect-like creatures. They can be found in the Grass Fields .

Appearance

They have a colorful iridescent exoskeleton with a leaf-shaped fin extending up from their backs.

Behavior

Leaf Bugs are always found running in small groups from the cover of one clump of foliage to another clump a short distance away. [ 1 ] They are passive, completely ignore pioneers, and do not take any damage.
