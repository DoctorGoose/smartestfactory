This shopping list of items is designed to answer the question: At each Milestone , what do I need to collect to unlock as much as possible in the MAM ?

  • Under each research node is the items required and the reward for unlocking if the node isn't its own reward e.g. The Rebar Gun .
    • If 100 or more of a required part needs to be found at crash sites, or a descendent node, they are listed below the line and are separate from the total to find e.g. SAM Fluctuator
  • Some Alternate Recipes require MAM nodes or additional milestones to be unlocked, these are listed under the alternate recipe e.g. Caterium Wire
  • Alternate recipes and research nodes are listed at the earliest possible stage they can be unlocked, not when they can be crafted. These have a reference saying why, and their shopping list needs are listed separately.
    • If any of the parts required to craft them need to be found at crash sites, they are italicized e.g. Blade Runners [ 1 ]
    • If any of the parts required to craft them are only available at higher Tiers, they are crossed out e.g.
  • Anything unlocked at an earlier milestone that can be crafted is not listed. E.g. after Tier 1 - Field Research , it is assumed you have a ready supply of Quartz Crystals .
  • Items that are required to obtain Hard Drives from Crash Sites are not factored into the sums listed here.
  • No guarantee is given that there are sufficient quantities of items around crash sites.
