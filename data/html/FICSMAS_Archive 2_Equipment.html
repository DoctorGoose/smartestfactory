The Candy Cane Basher is the FICSMAS variant of the Xeno-Basher . Both weapons are functionally identical, other than in appearance.

Unlocking

The Candy Cane Basher is unlocked via the FICSMAS Research chain in the MAM .

The Snowball Pile was the FICSMAS variant of the Nobelisk Detonator and used Snowballs as ammo.

As of Patch 0.5.1.3 , Snowballs can be loaded into the Nobelisk Detonator itself.

Crafting

The Snowball Pile could be crafted using 100× Actual Snow , 20× Candy Cane and 10× FICSMAS Bow .
