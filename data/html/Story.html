Prologue

The following prologue sets the stage for the game's narrative: [ 1 ]

In Satisfactory , you take the role of a Pioneer for FICSIT Incorporated , designated to the planet MASSAGE-2(A-B)b in the Akycha system, and to fulfill your obligations, you must exploit this planet for its resources by building factories, setting up power systems , organizing logistics, utilising vehicles , and asserting dominance over the local fauna using whatever tools you may engineer.

To advance FICSIT's mission, all notable phenomena, resources, technology, or other influence, should be investigated regardless of their perceived danger, as stated in the Pioneer contract. It is your duty to ensure they come to good use. On this mission, you are accompanied by ADA , your personal instance of the Artificial Directory and Assistant.

ADA will guide you throughout your mission, measuring your performance and providing you with helpful advice as you explore the planet and progress in your exploits.

Your first official objective is to initiate Project Assembly by building the Space Elevator . Project Assembly is divided into phases, each of which requires the delivery of Project Parts via the Space Elevator. Subsequent phases unlock access to new tiers , with more advanced technologies to construct, automate, explore and exploit.

But it's time to be honest: this project is part of the 'Save the Day' program - the largest united undertaking in human history, destined to save us all. Earth is in peril, and only FICSIT Pioneers offer hope for survival. FICSIT is counting on you.

Do not let FICSIT down.
