Vines

Primarily used for fuel. Biomass burners and vehicles can use it for power.

Item

Stack size

200

Vines used to be an organic item that could be harvested from certain plants. It could be used directly as fuel for Biomass Burners , but couldn't be turned into Biomass .

History

  • Unknown patch before Patch 0.1 : Completely removed
  • Patch Closed Alpha 4 : No longer attainable, although saves from before then may still have them in inventories
