Walker

Lifeform

Hitpoints

100

Damage

10

Behavior

Hostile

The Walker was a one-eyed cyclops-like monster, which could be found in a cave in Abyss Cliffs . There was only one of this creature on the map, and it didn't respawn upon reloading the game. Additionally, it did not drop any items upon death.

The enemy is from Sanctum 2 , another game by Coffee Stain Studios .

Behavior

  • The Walker made a creepy screeching sound when attacking. [ 1 ]
  • If it was approached by the pioneer , it would become aggressive. Its attack dealt 10 damage .
  • The monster triggered its attack by using both large hands.

Trivia

  • On April 1, 2021, the April Fools Special Event was introduced. This event repeats on the 1st of April every year. During the event, the Walker Graphics Mesh is used to replace the Fluffy-Tailed Hog Graphic Mesh.
